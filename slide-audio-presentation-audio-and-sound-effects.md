Enhance your presentations on AhaSlides with slide or presentation-level audio, along with sound effects for greater control over the experience.

Note: **Slide** and **Presentation Audio** are available only on **paid plans**. **Sound effects** are available on all plans.


# Audio


## How Audio Works

On AhaSlides, you can set audio to play on each slide as you present (**Slide Audio**) or throughout the entire presentation (**Presentation Audio**).

It is possible to have both **Presentation Audio** and **Slide Audio** set up in your presentation. The **Slide Audio** will **take priority** over the **Presentation Audio** when you present that slide.


## How to Set Up Slide Audio

- Select the slide on which you would like to play the audio and click the **Audio** section on the right side of the editing screen.
- Under the **Slide** tab, either click the dropdown menu to select a song from the AhaSlides audio library or press the **Upload** button to embed your own audio (mp3 format only with a maximum file size of 15mb).
- Once you have selected a slide audio track, you will see checkboxes for the audio settings: **Autoplay**, **On repeat**, **Playable on audience's devices**.

## How to Set Up Presentation Audio

- Select any slide in your presentation and click the **Audio** section on the right side of the editing screen.
- Under the **Presentation** tab, select a song from the dropdown menu.

# Sound effects


## How Sound effects work

Sound effects can play on both the presenter's and participants' sides, depending on the interaction.


## How to enable/disable Sound effect

- Select any slide in your presentation and click the **Audio** section on the right side of the editing screen.
- Under the **Presentation** tab, click on the toggles under **Sound effects**.

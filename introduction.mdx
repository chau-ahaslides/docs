## How AhaSlides Works

Getting started is straightforward. Follow these three steps to run your first live interactive session.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create">
    Build a presentation by adding interactive slide types — polls, quizzes, word clouds, Q&A, and more. Prefer to use existing content? Import a PowerPoint or PDF and layer interactivity on top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share">
    When your session begins, display the automatically generated QR code or join URL on screen. Your audience scans or types the link from any phone, tablet, or laptop — no app download needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engage">
    Audience members respond to each slide in real time. Votes, answers, and word-cloud entries appear live on the presenter screen the moment they come in, keeping every session dynamic and memorable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

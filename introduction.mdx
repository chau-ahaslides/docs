## How AhaSlides works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create">
    Build a presentation using AhaSlides' interactive slide types, or import your existing PowerPoint or PDF and insert interactive slides between your existing content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Present">
    Share your presentation at your event. Participants join via a URL or QR code on any phone, tablet, or laptop — no app download required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Respond">
    At each interactive slide, your audience responds in real time. Results appear live on the presenter's screen as votes and answers come in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

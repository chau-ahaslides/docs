## Interactive Slide Limits

The free plan allows up to 5 Quiz slides or 3 Poll-type slides per presentation while maintaining full 50-participant capacity. Exceeding either limit reduces the participant limit to 3 for that presentation.

<Warning>
  Adding more than 5 Quiz slides **OR** more than 3 Poll-type slides to a single presentation drops the participant capacity to **3** for that presentation only. Other presentations are not affected.
</Warning>

| Slide Category | Types | Free Plan Limit |
|---|---|---|
| Quiz | All quiz formats (Pick Answer, Type Answer, Match Pairs, Correct Order, Categorise) | 5 slides |
| Poll-type | Poll, Open Ended, Word Cloud, Brainstorm, Rating Scale | 3 slides |
| Other | Content, Spinner Wheel, Q&A, Ranking, Pin on Image, Idea Board, Diagram | Unlimited |

## How to Upgrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Pricing Page">
    Visit [ahaslides.com/pricing](https://ahaslides.com/pricing) and review the available plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Plan and Billing Cycle">
    Select the plan that fits your audience size and feature needs, then pick monthly or annual billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Checkout">
    Enter your payment details and confirm your purchase. Your plan upgrades immediately after payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Teachers, students, and NGOs can access discounted plans at [ahaslides.com/pricing/?plan=edu](https://ahaslides.com/pricing/?plan=edu).
</Tip>

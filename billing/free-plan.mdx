AhaSlides gives you a fully functional free plan that never expires — no trial period, no credit card required. You can create unlimited presentations, host live sessions with up to 50 participants, and use AI-powered slide generation from day one. The only restrictions are on how many interactive Quiz or Poll-type slides a single presentation can contain before a reduced participant cap kicks in.

## Interactive slide limits and the 3-participant rule

The free plan supports up to **5 Quiz slides** or **3 Poll-type slides** (Poll, Open-ended, Word Cloud, Brainstorm, and Rating Scale) in a single presentation. If your presentation stays within those limits, you can host events with the full 50-participant capacity at no cost.

<Warning>
  If a presentation contains **more than 5 Quiz slides or more than 3 Poll-type slides**, the participant limit for that presentation drops to **3 participants**.
</Warning>

The 3-participant fallback is intentional — it lets you test your presentation across multiple devices before a real event without requiring an upgrade. Use it to rehearse, check slide flow, and confirm that everything works exactly as you expect.

<Note>
  The 3-participant limit applies **per presentation**, based on its slide content. Other presentations in your account that stay within the limits are unaffected.
</Note>

## How to upgrade

If you need to host events with more than 50 participants, or want to use more interactive slides without the 3-participant fallback, an upgraded plan removes those restrictions entirely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the pricing page">
    Go to [ahaslides.com/pricing](https://ahaslides.com/pricing) to compare the Essential, Pro, and Enterprise plans side by side.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan and billing cycle">
    Select the plan that fits your audience size and choose between monthly or annual billing. Annual billing saves up to 67%.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Enter your payment details. Your account upgrades immediately after a successful payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you're a teacher, student, or work for an NGO, check out the discounted [Educational plans](https://ahaslides.com/pricing/?plan=edu) before purchasing a standard plan.
</Tip>

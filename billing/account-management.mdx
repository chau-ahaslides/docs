Your AhaSlides account settings give you control over your security, subscription, and data. From resetting a forgotten password to permanently deleting your account, this page walks you through each account management action step by step.

## Change or reset your password

### Change your password

Use this option when you know your current password and want to update it for security or personal preference.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to My Profile">
    Open [My Profile](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-profile) from your account menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Change password">
    Find the **Change password** section on the profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your passwords">
    Type your **Current Password** and then your **New password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Update password**. Your password is updated immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reset a forgotten password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the password reset page">
    Visit [presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/forgot-password](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/forgot-password).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address linked to your account using **lowercase letters only**. Capital letters can prevent the system from recognising your address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Reset Password">
    Click **Reset Password**. AhaSlides will send a reset link to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the email instructions">
    Open the email and follow the link to choose a new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log out from all devices

Use this option if you've signed in on a shared or public device and want to end all other active sessions instantly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to My Profile">
    Open [My Profile](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-profile).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Log out everywhere else">
    Find and click the **Log out everywhere else** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Log out**. All sessions on other devices and browsers are terminated immediately. Your current session remains active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account sharing policy

Each AhaSlides account is personal and must not be shared with other users. Sharing an account violates the [AhaSlides Terms of Use](https://ahaslides.com/terms/) and can lead to unexpected errors.

<Warning>
  If two presenters share a single account and one of them edits a presentation or changes slide questions between sessions, previous response data can be accidentally overwritten or lost.
</Warning>

If you need to collaborate with colleagues, use the built-in team and collaborative editing features instead — each person keeps their own account, and you work together without any of the risks that come from sharing credentials. See [Team Management](/billing/team-management) for more details.

[Sign up for a free account](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register) — it's free and takes less than a minute.

## Cancel your subscription

Cancelling a subscription turns off auto-renewal. Your plan remains active until the end of the period you've already paid for, and all your presentations stay in your account.

<Note>
  AhaSlides sends a **reminder email two weeks before** your subscription renews. You can cancel at any time — including right after purchase.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Via My Plan Page">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to My Plan">
        Open the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Cancel subscription">
        Click the **Cancel subscription** button.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose an option">
        You'll see two choices:
        - **Get extra free month** — receive 1 bonus month (monthly plan) or 2 bonus months (annual plan) instead of cancelling outright.
        - **Continue to cancel** — turns off auto-renewal. Your plan stays active until its expiry date.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Via Billing Page">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Billing settings">
        Click your profile image and select **Billing**, or click **Manage** in the Billing section on the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Cancel subscription">
        Click the **Cancel subscription** button on the Billing page to turn off auto-renewal.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### What happens after cancellation

- Your account reverts to the Free plan when your paid period ends.
- All existing presentations and response data remain in your account.
- You retain access to Free plan features but lose access to paid-only features.

## Delete your account

Deleting your account is permanent. Before you proceed, make sure you understand what is removed.

<Warning>
  **Deleting your account cannot be undone.** Your active subscription ends immediately, all presentations are permanently deleted, and all response data is lost.
</Warning>

If you want to keep your data but reduce costs, consider cancelling your subscription instead — your free account and all its content will remain intact.

### Delete your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to My Profile">
    Open [My Profile](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-profile).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Account deletion">
    Scroll down and choose **Account deletion**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete this account">
    Click **Delete this account**, then click **Continue deletion** on the confirmation prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with your email">
    Select a reason for deletion, then type the email address connected to your account to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finalise">
    Click **Delete account**. Your account is removed immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="I signed up with Google or Microsoft — how do I set a password?">
  If you registered using Google or Microsoft, your AhaSlides account doesn't have a separate password. You sign in through your Google or Microsoft account directly. To add a standalone password, contact [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change the email address on my account?">
  To change the email address linked to your account, go to [My Profile](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-profile) and update the email field. If you run into any issues, reach out to [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Will I lose my presentations if I cancel my subscription?">
  No. Cancelling only turns off auto-renewal. All your presentations and response data remain in your account, and you continue to have access to the Free plan features after your paid period ends.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get a refund after cancelling?">
  AhaSlides offers a full refund within 14 days of subscribing, provided you haven't used the platform at a live event. Email [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com) to request one. See the [Payment](/billing/payment) page for the full refund policy.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I recover a deleted account?">
  Account deletion is permanent and generally cannot be reversed. If you deleted your account recently and believe it was done in error, contact [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com) as soon as possible and the team will advise whether recovery is possible.
</Accordion>

AhaSlides processes all payments securely through **Stripe**, a globally trusted payment platform. You can pay with a credit or debit card, or use any account credit balance you've accumulated. Below you'll find everything you need to manage your payment details, retrieve invoices, and understand the refund policy.

## Accepted payment methods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Credit & Debit Cards" icon="credit-card">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Credit Balance" icon="wallet">
    Any credit balance in your AhaSlides account is applied automatically at checkout, reducing the amount charged to your card.
  </Card>

### How credit balance works

Your account accumulates a credit balance when you:

- **Downgrade your plan** — the unused portion of your current plan is converted to credit.
- **Refer a friend** — referral rewards are added as account credit.
- **Receive a gift balance** — AhaSlides may occasionally add promotional credit to your account.

You can view your current credit balance on the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page. The balance is deducted automatically whenever you purchase, upgrade, or renew a plan — no extra steps required. You can also see it reflected in the **Billing & Payments** section (click **Manage** on the My Plan page).

## Supported currencies

AhaSlides prices and charges in **USD**. If you pay with an international card, your bank automatically converts the amount to your local currency at the exchange rate it applies. The exact rate depends on your bank or card provider.

## How to apply a discount code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Go to [ahaslides.com/pricing](https://ahaslides.com/pricing), select the plan you want, and click **Buy Now** to reach the Checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the promotion code field">
    On the Checkout page, locate **Add promotion code** just below the **Subtotal** section and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter and apply your code">
    Type your discount code into the field that appears, then click **Apply**. The discounted total will update immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Failed payments

If your card is declined, it is usually caused by your bank's automated fraud-prevention systems rather than an issue with your card details.

**To resolve a failed payment:**

1. Double-check that the card number, expiry date, and CVV you entered are correct.
2. Try the same card again — transient bank-side blocks often clear within a short time.
3. Try an alternative credit or debit card if the first card continues to fail.

<Tip>
  Contact your bank to confirm the transaction was not blocked by their fraud-prevention systems. Most declined payments are a temporary bank-side restriction rather than a problem with your card details.
</Tip>

## Billing information and invoices

AhaSlides lets you download invoices and receipts directly from your account.

<Warning>
  Update your billing details **before** completing a purchase. Changes only apply to new invoices — existing invoices retain the information they were created with and cannot be amended retroactively.
</Warning>

<Note>
  AhaSlides is a **non-GST registered** company and does not collect tax from customers. Tax invoices are not available.
</Note>

### Update your billing information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing settings">
    Click your profile image and select **Billing**, or go to the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page and click **Manage**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your details">
    Scroll to the **Billing information** section and click **Update Information**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Fill in your billing details and click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Download an invoice or receipt

1. Navigate to your **Invoice History** in the Billing section.
2. Click the specific invoice you want to download.
3. On the next page, choose to download either the **invoice** or the **receipt**.

### Update your billing address

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to My Profile">
    Open [My Profile](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-profile) from your account menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your address">
    Scroll to the **Billing Address** section and enter your details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Update profile** to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Refund policy

AhaSlides offers a **14-day full refund** on new subscriptions, subject to the conditions below.

| Condition | Eligible for refund? |
|---|---|
| Within 14 days of subscribing **and** haven't used AhaSlides at a live event | ✅ Full refund |
| Within 14 days of subscribing **but** have used AhaSlides at a live event | ❌ Not eligible |
| After 14 days of subscribing | ❌ Not eligible |

To request a refund, email [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com). If you meet the criteria, AhaSlides will process the refund immediately, and the amount will appear in your bank account within **5–10 business days**, depending on your bank.

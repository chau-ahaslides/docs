## Annual vs. monthly billing

All paid plans are available on both monthly and annual billing cycles. Annual billing is charged upfront for a full year and saves **up to 67%** compared to paying month by month.

| Billing cycle | How it works | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **Monthly** | Charged every month, auto-renews until you cancel | Short-term or infrequent use |
| **Annual** | Charged once per year, auto-renews after 12 months | Regular use — most cost-effective |

<Tip>
  You can turn off auto-renewal for any plan at any time from your [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page. Your access continues until the end of the period you've already paid for.
</Tip>

### Monthly plan

A monthly plan renews automatically every month on your billing date. If you only need one month, purchase the plan and then turn off auto-renewal before the next billing date to avoid a second charge.

### Annual plan

The annual plan (also called the Yearly plan) is the most cost-effective option for anyone using AhaSlides regularly throughout the year — for workshops, training sessions, employee meetings, events, and more. After purchase, you can cancel auto-renewal at any time from your [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page.

## One-time plans

If you only need AhaSlides for a single event, a One-Time plan is a cost-efficient alternative to a monthly subscription.

<Note>
  A One-Time plan **activates when your presentation reaches 8 participants** and stays active for **24 hours** from that point. You can run unlimited presentations during that 24-hour window.
</Note>

You can purchase a One-Time plan in advance and prepare your presentation without triggering the 24-hour window — the countdown only starts when an 8th participant joins. If you're on an existing paid plan and need a larger audience for a single event, a One-Time plan can be layered on top; after the 24 hours, your account reverts to your standard plan automatically.

Purchase a One-Time plan on the **One-time** tab at [ahaslides.com/pricing](https://ahaslides.com/pricing).

## Educational plans

Teachers, students, and non-profit or non-governmental organisations can access discounted plans billed annually.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Educational pricing page">
    Visit [ahaslides.com/pricing/?plan=edu](https://ahaslides.com/pricing/?plan=edu) to see available Edu plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your plan">
    Click **Buy Now** on the plan that fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage auto-renewal">
    After purchase, consider turning off auto-renewal right away on the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page if you only want one year.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To return to standard plans from the Edu pricing page, click **Back to standard plans** at the top of the page.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?">
  Yes. You can switch your billing cycle when your current plan period ends. Visit [ahaslides.com/pricing](https://ahaslides.com/pricing) or go to your [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page and select a new plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my presentations if I downgrade?">
  All your existing presentations and response data remain in your account after a downgrade. You will still have access to free plan features but will lose access to paid-only features.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel at any time?">
  Yes. Cancelling turns off auto-renewal — your plan stays active until the end of the period you've paid for. You can cancel from the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page or through the **Billing** section in your profile.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial for paid plans?">
  AhaSlides doesn't offer a time-limited free trial, but the free plan is available indefinitely with no credit card required. If you upgrade and change your mind, you can request a full refund within 14 days, provided you haven't used AhaSlides at a live event.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do Educational plans have all the same features as standard plans?">
  Educational plans are discounted versions of the standard plans and are billed annually. They include the same features as their standard counterparts at the equivalent tier.
</Accordion>

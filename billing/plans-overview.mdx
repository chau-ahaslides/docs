AhaSlides offers plans for individuals, teams, and large organisations — from a free plan with no expiry to enterprise licensing with white-label options. Choose based on your audience size, feature needs, and how often you present.

## Plan Types

| Plan | Best For | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | Anyone getting started | Up to 50 participants, unlimited presentations, 5 Quiz / 3 Poll-type slide limit, AI features, collaborative editing. No credit card, no expiry. |
| **Essential** | Regular presenters needing larger audiences | Higher participant limits, more interactive slides per presentation. |
| **Pro** | Power users and professionals | Unlimited interactive slides, custom branding, Presenter role for collaborators, priority support, permanent links, custom access codes. |
| **Enterprise** | Organisations with custom needs | Custom licensing, white-label branding, SSO, dedicated support. Contact [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com) for a quote. |
| **Educational** | Teachers, students, and NGOs | Same features as standard plans at discounted prices. See [ahaslides.com/pricing/?plan=edu](https://ahaslides.com/pricing/?plan=edu). |

## Annual vs. Monthly Billing

| Billing Cycle | How It Works | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly | Renews automatically each month | Short-term or infrequent use |
| Annual | Billed upfront once per year — saves up to 67% | Regular presenters; most cost-effective |

## One-Time Event Plans

AhaSlides offers one-time plans for single events. These are ideal when you need a paid feature set for a specific occasion without committing to a recurring subscription.

- **Activation**: The plan activates when your presentation reaches 8 participants.
- **Duration**: Remains active for 24 hours from that point.
- **Coverage**: Covers unlimited presentations during the 24-hour window.
- **Stacking**: Can be layered on top of an existing paid plan for extra participant capacity.

## Enterprise & White Label

Enterprise licensing is available for organisations needing custom participant limits, white-label branding (removes all AhaSlides branding), SSO integration, and dedicated account support. Email [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com) to discuss options and receive a tailored quote.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch from monthly to annual?">
    Yes. When your current billing period ends, you can switch cycles from the **My Plan** page. The new billing cycle takes effect at the next renewal date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my presentations if I downgrade?">
    All presentations and data are retained when you downgrade. You lose access to paid features, but your content remains completely safe and accessible on the free plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel at any time?">
    Yes. Cancelling stops renewal at the end of the current billing period. You keep full access to your paid features until that date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    There is no time-limited trial, but the free plan is permanent and requires no credit card. A 14-day refund policy applies to new paid subscriptions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do educational plans have the same features as standard plans?">
    Yes. Educational plans include the same features as their standard counterparts — just at a discounted price for eligible users.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

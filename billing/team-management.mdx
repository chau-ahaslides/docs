AhaSlides teams let multiple presenters collaborate under a shared workspace, with a single owner managing membership and plan licenses. You can start a team for free and upgrade to a paid Team Plan when you need members to access premium features. This page covers everything from creating your first team to sharing individual presentations with specific colleagues.

## Free team vs. Team Plan

There are two distinct team modes in AhaSlides:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free Team" icon="users">
    The owner or admin can add up to **200 members**. Members only have access to the Free plan features unless they upgrade their own individual accounts separately.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Plan" icon="rocket">
    The owner purchases a set number of **licenses**. Each invited member who accepts automatically receives access to the plan tier (Essential or Pro) that the owner purchased — at no extra cost to the member.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  A team owner can hold their own paid individual plan while the team remains on the Free plan. These are treated as **two separate plan types** and do not interfere with each other.
</Note>

## Creating a team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Team Member prompt">
    In the left-side panel of your AhaSlides dashboard, click the **Add Team Member** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your team and add a member">
    Enter a team name and add at least one person. Choose their role — **Member** or **Admin** — before sending the invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your team dashboard">
    After creation, you'll be taken to the Team Member dashboard where you can manage everyone going forward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Once a team is created, the team name **cannot be changed** from within the app. To update your team name, email [hi@ahaslides.com](mailto:hi@ahaslides.com) from the team owner's email address.
</Warning>

## Inviting members and assigning roles

Go to the [Team Members](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/team/members) page and click the blue **Invite member** button. Enter one or more email addresses and select a role from the dropdown before sending.

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full control: invite, remove, change roles, manage the plan and licenses |
| **Admin** | Invite and remove Admins and Members; all Member permissions |
| **Member** | View, edit, and present any presentation in the team workspace |

<Note>
  On a **Team Plan**, one license is deducted each time an invited member accepts their invitation. That member's account is automatically upgraded to the team's plan tier. If they don't yet have an AhaSlides account, they'll be prompted to create one.
</Note>

You can track pending invitations in the **Invitations** tab. To withdraw an invitation that hasn't been accepted yet, click the **X** icon next to it.

## Changing member roles

As the **Owner**, you can switch any member between the **Member** and **Admin** roles at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Team Members dashboard">
    Click **Members** under your team name in the left-hand panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the role dropdown">
    Find the member whose role you want to change, then open the **Role** dropdown next to their name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the new role">
    Choose **Admin** or **Member**. The change takes effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the member on the dashboard">
    Find the member's name in the Team Members list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Remove from team">
    Click the **X** icon labelled **Remove from team** on the far right of their row.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  On a **Team Plan**, removing a member **returns one license to your pool**, making it available for someone new. The removed member's account automatically reverts to the Free plan.
</Note>

If a **team owner leaves**, the entire team is deleted. All members lose access to any presentations and folders shared specifically with the team.

## Purchasing and managing licenses

If you need more members on a paid Team Plan, you can purchase additional licenses from three places:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pricing Page">
    Go to [ahaslides.com/pricing](https://ahaslides.com/pricing), scroll to the Team Plan section, select your plan tier and adjust the quantity of licenses, then click **Buy Now**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="My Plan Page">
    Go to [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan), scroll to the Team Plan section, choose a plan and license count, then click **Buy Now**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team Members Page">
    Go to the [Team Members](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/team/members) page and click **Upgrade team** or **Upgrade for more** to be taken to the My Plan page.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Changing your license count

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing settings">
    Click your profile image and select **Billing**, or click **Manage** in the Billing section on the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Update subscription">
    Select **Update subscription** on the Billing page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust quantity">
    Increase or decrease the license count as needed, then click **Continue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Confirm** to apply the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you're **reducing** your license count, you must **remove enough team members first** so that your active team size matches the new lower count before confirming the change.
</Warning>

## Team workspace

The team workspace is a shared area visible to all members of your team. Any presentation you move into the team workspace becomes accessible to every team member — they can view, edit, and present it based on their assigned role.

Presentations in a member's **Personal Workspace** (the **My Presentations** tab) are private and not visible to the rest of the team unless explicitly shared.

## Sharing a presentation with specific members

You don't have to share a presentation with your entire team. You can send a personal copy to selected individuals.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From the Editor">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Share menu">
        Inside the presentation editor, click **Share** in the top toolbar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send a copy">
        Under **Send a copy**, select **Send to…**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose recipients">
        Check the box next to each team member you want to send a copy to, then click **Send**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From the Dashboard">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the 3-dot menu">
        On the dashboard, click the **⋯** menu on the right side of the presentation name.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Share">
        Select **Share** from the dropdown menu, then follow the same steps as above.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

When sending a copy, you can optionally include:

- **Slide notes** — sends the presenter notes attached to each slide.
- **Response data** — includes all audience responses, emoji reactions, and Q&A questions from previous sessions.

The copy arrives in the recipient's **Personal Workspace** under **My Presentations**, marked with a green icon to indicate it was sent to them.

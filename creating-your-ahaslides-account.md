Welcome to AhaSlides! Follow these simple steps to create your AhaSlides account, using the method that works best for you.


## 1. Create AhaSlides Account by Entering Your Email

- Go to the [sign-up page](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register).
- Enter your email and choose a password.
- Enter your full name.
- Press **Sign Up**.

## 2. Using Your Microsoft Account

- Go to the [sign-up page](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register).
- Choose the **Sign up with Microsoft** option.
- Select the email account you would like to sign up with.
- Sign in.

## 3. Sign Up by Connecting with Your Google Account

- Go to the [sign-up page](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register).
- Choose the **Sign up with Google** option.
- Select the email account you would like to sign up with.
- Sign in.

## 4. Sign Up Using Single Sign-On (SSO)

- Go to the [sign-up page](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register).
- Choose the **Sign up with SSO** option.
- Enter your organisation's email address.
- Press **Log In**.
If you want to use Single Sign-On (SSO) for your team, please contact our sales team via this form: [https://ahaslides.com/enterprise/](https://ahaslides.com/enterprise/)

Start your journey with AhaSlides today and make your presentations more interactive and engaging.

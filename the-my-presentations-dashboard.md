'My Presentations' is your dashboard in AhaSlides. This is where you can see and manage all of your presentations.

Welcome to **My Presentations**. This is what you see the first time you log in to AhaSlides.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn1945c8f3231b7d6ceed763ddc2302d0dd402601bc2570e0ff0be27e716120127987fbfc191f02b7dfd6558dc71650ef0?inline=true)

# Creating a New Presentation

1. To create your first presentation, click the button labelled '**New presentation +**'
2. Next, give your presentation a name, and if you want, a customized access code.
3. You will be taken directly to the editor, where you can start to edit your presentation.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn97ab0867907473455107fae2234816050f4be613c3df8bb2a5d7296c066087a90b3d45567cb72b97906d84566cc1cc0f?inline=true)

# Sorting Presentations into Folders

You can sort your presentations into folders for better organisation.

1. Click the button labelled **'+ New Folder'**
2. Give your folder a name.
3. Select the existing presentations that you would like to move to that folder, then click the '**Move to**' button.
4. You can also create a new presentation from within the folder itself

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn998aef1093205b1ffda69eb9a372573f01d4749823d4f18a96d9914cea1ba62f3f9d401e2187ea75d184d061d3ec23e1?inline=true)

# Organizing Sub-folders

> Each folder can contain up to 200 presentations.
> Subfolders are now supported in up to 10 levels.

To maintain your presentation list well organized, you can create sub-folders within a folder:

1. Choose the folder in which you want to create a sub-folder and click **'+ New Folder'**
2. Assign a name to your sub-folder
3. Select the presentations you wish to move to the sub-folder and click the **'Move to'** button.
4. Additionally, you can create a new presentation directly within the sub-folder

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn9f19c63d22858b644ba410f69558b2cb2d3134d6ffe8ab14ff7eb8233d9f62c44ea3007efe5071401233a1c653066cc2?inline=true)

# The Chat Bubble

The blue chat bubble in the bottom-right corner of 'My Presentations' connects you directly with our support team, who are always ready to answer any of your questions.

'My Presentations' is your dashboard in AhaSlides. This is where you can see and manage all of your presentations.


# Creating a New Presentation

- To create your first presentation, click the button labelled '**New presentation +'**
- Next, give your presentation a name, and if you want, a customized access code.
- You will be taken directly to the editor, where you can start to edit your presentation.

# Sorting Presentations into Folders

You can sort your presentations into folders for better organisation.

- Click the button labelled **'+ New Folder'**
- Give your folder a name.
- Select the existing presentations that you would like to move to that folder, then click the '**Move to**' button.
- You can also create a new presentation from within the folder itself

# Organizing Sub-folders

Each folder can contain up to 200 presentations. Subfolders are now supported in up to 10 levels.

- Choose the folder in which you want to create a sub-folder and click **'+ New Folder'**
- Assign a name to your sub-folder
- Select the presentations you wish to move to the sub-folder and click the **'Move to'** button.

# The Chat Bubble

The blue chat bubble in the bottom-right corner of 'My Presentations' connects you directly with our support team, who are always ready to answer any of your questions.

# AhaSlides Template Library

The AhaSlides Template Library is a time-saving feature that allows you to download and customize ready-made templates for your presentations.

## Accessing the template library

1. **Log in to your AhaSlides account**: Once logged in, navigate to the left-side panel.
2. **Choose the Templates category**: In the menu, you'll find the 'Templates' category. Click on it to browse through a variety of pre-made templates.

## Downloading a template

1. **Browse the list of templates**: Once in the Template Library, you'll see a list of templates designed for various purposes.
2. **Preview the slides**: Click on a template to see a summary of the slides it contains.
3. **Download the template**: Click the **Get Template** button to download the template. It will automatically be added to your list of presentations, and the editor will open with the template ready for you to start working on.

## Searching for specific templates

- **Use the search bar**: If you're looking for something specific, just type your keywords in the search bar to find the template that matches your needs.

**📣 Important Note:** Any downloaded template will also include response data. Remember to reset the results before you use it. Find out how to reset results [here](resetting-the-results.md).

## Start editing or presenting

Once the template is added to your list, you can either start editing the slides to fit your presentation goals or use it as is for your session.

The AhaSlides Template Library will be growing all the time, so remember to check back regularly for new presentations that can save you a lot of time.

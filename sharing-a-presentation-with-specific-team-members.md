---

Sharing a presentation in the Team Workspace makes it accessible to everyone in your team, but here's how to **share it with specific members of your team**, as opposed to the *whole* team.


## From the Editor

Click on the presentation you would like to share. On the editor....

- Head to the **Share** menu in the top toolbar
- Under **Send a copy**, select **Send to...**
Here you will see a list of the people in your team. Simply check the box of the member (or members) you would like to send the presentation to, then click **Send**.

Some of the other features here are:

- **Include slide notes**. Check to send the slide notes along with the presentation.
- **Include response data**. Check to send all responses, emoji reactions, and Q&A questions from your audience.

## From the Dashboard

You can also do this from the dashboard by clicking the 3-dot menu on the right of the presentation name, then clicking **Share**.


## Where does the Presentation go?

Once you've sent a copy of your presentation to your team member(s), it will arrive in their **Personal Workspace** in the usual **My Presentations** tab.

You can purchase AhaSlides plans with cards and credit balance. Your payment is handled securely by **Stripe** - our global trusted payment partner.


## Card payment

AhaSlides accepts **international credit cards and debit cards**. When you make a purchase, the upgrade process is automatic and happens with immediate effect.


## Credit balance

When you downgrade, refer a friend (or receive balance as a gift), your account will have a credit balance. This balance can be viewed in the **[My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan)** page.

This balance can be used in:

- Upgrading your plan.
- Purchase a plan.
- Renewing your plan.
To use the balance, you only need to make a purchase/upgrade normally, and the balance will be deducted from the price of the plan that you would like to purchase.

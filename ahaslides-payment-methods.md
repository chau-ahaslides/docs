# AhaSlides payment methods

You can purchase AhaSlides plans with cards and credit balance. Your payment is handled securely by **Stripe** - our global trusted payment partner.

## Card payment

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn9e615506fd8641b1c549a091992bef3ce0c8f6d668f7d2da62fa7ae1221745a90d3e63ff68a4fc6458a1b41c5c4a0711?inline=true)

AhaSlides accepts **international credit cards and debit cards**. When you make a purchase, the upgrade process is automatic and happens with immediate effect.

## Credit balance

When you downlgrade, refer a friend (or receive balance as a gift), your account will have a credit balance. This balance can be viewed in the **[My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan)** page

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnc9c6685f77ab15f3173ddf9c5442d47e31e539cd3a1ad6968e83e45faeb2f351469b1073752093795765bb21ad8c9f42?inline=true)

This balance can be used in:

- Upgrading your plan.
- Purchase a plan.
- Renewing your plan.

To use the balance, you only need to make purchase/upgrade normally, and the balance will be deducted from the price of the plan that you would like to purchase:

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnc9c6685f77ab15f3173ddf9c5442d47e9e8330130f054b946a8bd5a96b57923b8fd98a01f506eab8de433306aabbdbb1?inline=true)

The same applies for renewing your plan. You can see the information in the **Billing & Payments** page (by clicking **Manage** on the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page):

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn598ea68a676f302120094a60a6beb93f11a9691d78eb5cd5ac2888555d48a69fee142d4009d7b9327782a2da61904676?inline=true)

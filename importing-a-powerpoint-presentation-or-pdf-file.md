You can import a PowerPoint presentation to AhaSlides in PPT, PPTX or PDF format. Doing this means you can put interactive slides from AhaSlides in between the slides of your PowerPoint.


# How to import your files

By importing your PowerPoint to AhaSlides, you can **insert any of AhaSlides interactive slides into your PowerPoint presentation**.

You can import any PowerPoint presentation with a maximum of **50MB and 100 slides**.


## Where can you find the import button:

There are **two ways** you can import your PowerPoint file to AhaSlides:


### 1. Import on the AhaSlides Dashboard

On the **My Presentations** dashboard, you can click the button labelled 'Import'. Once you've done this, a pop-up window will ask you to browse your computer for your PPT, PPTX or PDF PowerPoint file. You can also drag and drop your file on to the pop-up window.


### 2. Import on the presentation editor

On the AhaSlides presentation editor, you can import your PowerPoint into your existing presentation. Simply go to the AhaSlides presentation in which you want to add your PowerPoint, then click the button labelled '**Import**'.


## Import options

After selecting/inserting the file to upload, you will have 3 options:

- **Import and generate slides with AI**: Import your file as slides, then AI will suggest and insert interactive ones after relevant sections.
- **Generate interactive slides**: Use AI to create interactive slides from your file content, without importing the originals.
- **Import slides**: Import your file exactly as it is.

# What Happens to an Imported PowerPoint/PDF?

Once imported into your AhaSlides presentation, each slide of your PowerPoint will be displayed as a separate Content slide. These slides will contain **static images** of each of your PowerPoint slides, so **animations, embedded audio, and links will not be imported into AhaSlides**.


# Making a PowerPoint Interactive

While you can't make the PowerPoint slides interactive themselves, you can [generate slides using AI](https://help.ahaslides.com/portal/en/kb/articles/generating-quiz-questions-from-imported-ppt-or-pdf-files-on-ahaslides), or **place AhaSlides interactive slides in between the slides of your PowerPoint**.

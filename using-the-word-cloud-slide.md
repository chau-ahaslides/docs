A word cloud lets you pose a question and have participants submit short written responses. All the responses are displayed on one screen, with the most popular responses appearing larger in the cloud.

The most popular response will be the largest one in the cloud and will sit directly in the middle of all other responses.


## Setting up your Word Cloud


#### Step 1: Create your slide and question

Start by creating a new slide and selecting the word cloud slide type from the options. Write your question or statement in the 'your question' box.


#### Step 2: Change your word cloud settings

Change the settings of your word cloud to your liking. There are 6 settings that you can change:

- **Entries per participant**: The amount of responses that each of your participants is allowed to submit. This can be between 1 and 10.
- **Time limit**: This applies a time limit to the slide.
- **Close submission**: Close submissions for specific slides.
- **Hide results**: This hides the submitted responses from the presenter's screen as they're being submitted.
- **Allow audience to submit more than once**: This allows each participant to submit their responses an unlimited amount of times.
- **Filter profanity**: This turns on the profanity filter.

### Step 3: Present and get responses

Once you press the 'Present' button, your participants will be able to submit their responses to your word cloud.


## Smart Grouping your Word Clouds

On AhaSlides, you have the capability to intelligently group word clouds. With Smart Grouping, words with similar meanings are clustered together.

To group responses within a Word Cloud slide, follow these steps:

- While presenting, navigate to the bottom of the presenter's screen.
- Locate and click on the 'Group' icon.
The Word Cloud slide supports a maximum of 5,000 submissions.

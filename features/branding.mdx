AhaSlides lets you personalise the look of every presentation with custom fonts and your own logo. On Pro and Enterprise plans, you can replace or remove the default AhaSlides branding so every slide reflects your organisation's visual identity.

<Note>
  Custom fonts and logo management are available on **Pro and Enterprise plans** only.
</Note>

---

## Custom fonts

Change the font across your entire presentation with one action, and build a personal library of fonts for easy reuse across all your presentations.

### Changing the font for a presentation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Design tab">
    In the editor, select any slide and go to the **Design** tab in the right-hand panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Text">
    Scroll down to the **Text** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a font">
    Use the dropdown menu to choose a font. The change applies to your **entire presentation** and is visible on your audience's screens too.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Changing the font on one slide updates the font across all slides in the presentation.
</Note>

### Managing your font library

AhaSlides gives you access to a library of **250 fonts**. You can curate your own shortlist so your preferred fonts are always one click away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the font library">
    In the **Design** tab, click the font dropdown under **Text**, then click **More fonts** at the top of the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your fonts">
    Check the checkbox next to any font you want to save. Selected fonts appear in the **My fonts** column on the right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Done">
    Click **Done**. Your selected fonts are now available in the **Text** dropdown across **all presentations in your account**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Building your font library once means you never have to scroll through all 250 fonts again. Your saved fonts appear at the top of the dropdown on every future presentation.
</Tip>

### Fonts on Content slides

Content slides have their own small font toolbar that appears when you edit text. To apply a font from your managed library to a Content slide, go to the **Design** tab and select the font from the **Text** dropdown — this overrides the Content slide's default font.

<Warning>
  It is currently not possible to import or upload custom font files to AhaSlides. All fonts must be selected from the built-in library of 250 fonts.
</Warning>

## Logo management

By default, the AhaSlides logo appears in the top-right corner of every slide and is visible on both the presenter screen and participants' devices. On Pro and Enterprise plans, you can hide it or replace it with your own logo.

### Hiding the logo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover over the logo">
    In the editor, hover over the logo in the top-right corner of the presentation canvas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Hide">
    Click the logo, then select **Hide**. The logo disappears from all slides in your presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restore the logo if needed">
    To bring the logo back, hover over the top-right corner where the logo used to be and click **Unhide**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Replacing the logo with your own

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover over the logo">
    In the editor, hover over the logo in the top-right corner of the canvas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Change">
    Click the logo, then select **Change**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Upload the image or GIF file you want to use as your logo. It appears in the top-right corner of all slides immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset to AhaSlides logo if needed">
    To restore the original AhaSlides logo, hover over your logo and click **Reset**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Any change you make to the logo — hiding, changing, or resetting — applies immediately across all slides in your presentation. You do not need to update each slide individually.
</Note>

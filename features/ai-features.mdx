AhaSlides includes built-in AI tools that help you build engaging presentations faster. You can generate quiz questions and answer options from a prompt, or let AI read an uploaded file and turn it into interactive slides automatically. Both tools are available on all plans.

## Generating questions and answers with AI

When you add a quiz or poll slide, AhaSlides AI can suggest questions based on your presentation name and generate ready-to-use answer options for any question you type. This saves you the manual work of brainstorming options and helps you get a complete quiz slide in seconds.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Generate questions" icon="circle-question">
    AI suggests questions automatically based on your presentation name when you add any quiz slide.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Generate answer options" icon="list-check">
    Type your own question, then click **Generate options** to let AI produce suitable answer choices instantly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### How to generate a question

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a presentation">
    Start a new presentation and give it a descriptive name. AhaSlides AI uses the presentation name to suggest relevant questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a quiz slide">
    Add any quiz slide type to your presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a generated question">
    Under **Your Question**, AI-generated suggestions appear automatically. Click any suggestion to use it as your question.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  AI question generation is available on all quiz slide types.
</Note>

### How to generate answer options

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a quiz or poll slide">
    Create any quiz or poll slide in your presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type your question">
    In the **Content** tab, type the question you want to ask your audience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Generate options">
    Click the **Generate options** button. AI reads your question and produces a set of suitable answer choices to choose from.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  AI answer generation is available on all quiz and poll slides.
</Note>

## Generating interactive slides from imported files

If you already have a PowerPoint, PDF, image, or document file, you can upload it and let AI turn the content into interactive slides. You do not need to write questions manually — AI reads the file and creates slides based on what it finds.

### How to generate slides from a file

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Import button">
    On the **My Presentations** dashboard or inside the presentation editor, click the **Import** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your file">
    Click the upload area to browse for your file, or drag and drop it onto the window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a generation option">
    After selecting your file, choose how you want to use it:

    | Option | What it does |
    |---|---|
    | **Import and generate slides with AI** | Imports your file as slides, then AI suggests and inserts interactive slides after relevant sections |
    | **Generate interactive slides** | Uses AI to create interactive slides from your file content, without importing the original slides |
    | **Import slides** | Imports the file exactly as it is, with no AI generation (you can generate slides manually later) |

    For document files, only **Generate interactive slides** is available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Start">
    Click **Start** to begin the import and generation process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use **Import and generate slides with AI** to keep your original slide content and automatically add quiz questions at the end of each section — ideal for training materials and educational decks.
</Tip>

## Plan availability

Both AI generation tools — question and answer generation, and slide generation from files — are available on **all plans**, including Free.

AhaSlides offers several ways to work with others on your presentations. You can invite collaborators to edit or present a shared presentation, share an entire folder, or send a copy of a presentation to another AhaSlides user. This page covers all three collaboration methods.

<Note>
  Collaborating with members of your team plan is available on all plans. Inviting people **outside** your team as collaborators is supported on Pro, Edu Large, and Enterprise plans (and currently available to all users during a promotional period).
</Note>

<Warning>
  Features of the plan owner are not shared through collaboration. A collaborator uses their own plan's feature set, not the owner's. If you need paid features for multiple users, consider purchasing multiple licences on a Team plan.
</Warning>

## Collaborating on a presentation

Add collaborators to a presentation and assign them either the **Editor** or **Presenter** role.

### Adding collaborators by email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invitation menu">
    In the presentation editor, click the icon showing your profile image and a **+** sign in the toolbar. Alternatively, click the **Share** menu button and choose the **Add Collaborators** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email addresses">
    Type the email addresses of the people you want to invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a role">
    Choose **Editor** or **Presenter** for each person. Editors can edit and present the presentation. Presenters can only view and present it (see the [Presenter role](#the-presenter-role) section below for details).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Each collaborator receives an email notifying them of their access. You remain the owner of the presentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Inviting editors via a shareable link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Share menu">
    In the editor, click the **Share** menu button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on Invite editors via link">
    Toggle on **Invite editors via link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy and share the link">
    Click **Copy** and send the link to your collaborators. When they open the link and sign in, they gain Editor access automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The **Invite editors via link** option does not support the Presenter role. Use email invitations to assign the Presenter role.
</Note>

### Editing and viewing together

Only one person can actively edit or present a presentation at a time. When a collaborator is viewing the same presentation you are editing, their profile picture appears in the top toolbar.

If you are viewing while someone else is editing, you see a bar at the top of the editor with two options:
- **Take over** — makes you the editor immediately and moves the current editor to viewer mode
- **Refresh** — reloads the page so you can see the latest changes

## Collaborating on a folder

You can share entire folders with collaborators, giving them access to all presentations inside.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the folder">
    On the **My Presentations** page, find the folder you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open folder options">
    Click the **3-dot button** to the right of the folder, or right-click the folder, and choose **Add Collaborators**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add collaborators">
    Enter the collaborators' email addresses, select a role, and click **Add**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Moving presentations between folders

<Note>
  You can move a presentation to a folder only if you have edit access to the source folder, the presentation itself, and the destination folder.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select presentations">
    On the **My Presentations** page, check the checkbox next to the presentation thumbnail. Select multiple presentations if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Move to">
    Click **Move to** in the toolbar at the bottom of the page. You can also right-click a presentation to find **Move to**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the destination">
    Select the destination folder and click **Move**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Finding shared presentations and folders

- As the **owner**, shared presentations and folders still appear in your **My Presentations** tab alongside everything else you created.
- As a **collaborator**, any presentation or folder you were invited to appears in the **Shared with Me** tab.

To leave a shared presentation or folder, right-click it or click its 3-dot button, then choose **Leave**.

## The Presenter role

The Presenter role lets multiple people run the same presentation independently at the same time, each with their own join link, QR code, and session report. This is ideal for team training, conferences, or any situation where several facilitators use the same content simultaneously.

<Note>
  The Presenter role requires a **Pro plan**. It is not supported on the PowerPoint add-in.
</Note>

### What a Presenter can do

A collaborator assigned the Presenter role can:
- View the presentation
- Present the presentation independently
- Reset results after their session
- View and export the report for their own sessions only

A Presenter **cannot** edit the presentation. When you update the presentation as the owner, Presenters can click **Get the latest** to receive the updated version.

### Inviting collaborators as Presenter

Follow the same steps as adding collaborators by email (above), and choose **Presenter** as the role instead of Editor.

### Viewing session reports by presenter

As the owner, you can view reports for every session run by you and your collaborators.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the report page">
    Click the **Report** button in the editor toolbar, or hover over the presentation on the dashboard and click the purple **Report** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Session list">
    Click the **Session list** box next to the owner's name. A window lists every session run by you and your Presenter collaborators.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a session">
    Click any session to view its report. Each reset of the presentation's results creates a new session entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sharing a copy of a presentation

Send a complete copy of your presentation to another AhaSlides user so they can use and edit it independently in their own account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Share menu">
    In the presentation editor, click **Share** in the top bar and choose the **Share Slides** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the share link">
    Click **Copy link** and share the link wherever you want — by email, chat, or any other method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set optional permissions">
    Click the **3-dot button** next to **Copy link** to enable additional sharing options:
    - **Include Slide Notes** — recipients can view your speaker notes when they copy the presentation
    - **Allow Comments** — anyone with the link can leave comments on the shared presentation
  </Step>
</Steps>

When a logged-in AhaSlides user opens the link, they see an option to copy the presentation to their account. If they are not logged in, they are prompted to sign up or log in first.

<Note>
  Audience response data from the original presentation is **not** copied. The recipient receives only the slides and content. They can edit the presentation freely in their own account.
</Note>

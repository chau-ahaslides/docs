# What is Included in the Free Account?

AhaSlides offers a free plan with access to a range of features for as long as you like. Here's what you can enjoy with our free plan:

1. **Up to fifty live participants:** Engage with up to 50 live participants during your presentations.
2. **No monthly participant limit:** You can have as many participants as you like, as long as 50 or fewer participants join your quiz simultaneously.
3. **Unlimited presentations per month:** There's no limit to the number of presentations you can create and use.
4. **Up to five Quiz or three Collect opinions/Q&A slides:** Create up to 5 Quiz slides or 3 Poll-type (Poll, Open-ended, Word cloud, Brainstorm, and Rating scale) slides. Please refer to the notes below if you exceed this limit.
5. **AI features:** Use free AI assistance to generate engaging slides tailored to your needs.
6. **Collaborative editing:** Collaborate with friends and colleagues by allowing them to edit the presentation with you.
7. **PowerPoint Integrations**: Insert interactive AhaSlides slides directly into your existing PowerPoint presentations with our PowerPoint Add-in
8. **PowerPoint/PDF import:** Import your existing PPT, PPTX, and PDF files from PowerPoint or elsewhere into an AhaSlides presentation.

> **Note:** If your presentation has **more than 5 Quiz or 3 Poll-type slides**, you'll only be able to host an event with up to **3 participants.**
>
> We've kept this 3-participant limit so that you can still test your presentation across different devices before your main event. This way, you can be extra confident that everything will run smoothly when it matters most!
>
> For events with **under 50 participants** that only need **up to 5 Quiz or 3 Poll-type slides**, AhaSlides is completely **free to use**! And there's no cap on the number of participants per month, so you can host as many events as you need.
>
> If you need to host larger events or use more quiz or question slides, consider checking out our upgraded plans for more flexibility: [Pricing page](https://ahaslides.com/pricing/)

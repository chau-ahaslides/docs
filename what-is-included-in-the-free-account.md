title: What is Included in the Free Account?
status: Published
---

AhaSlides offers a free plan with access to a range of features for as long as you like. Here's what you can enjoy with our free plan:

- **Up to fifty live participants:** Engage with up to 50 live participants during your presentations.
- **No monthly participant limit:** You can have as many participants as you like, as long as 50 or fewer participants join your quiz simultaneously.
- **Unlimited presentations per month:** There's no limit to the number of presentations you can create and use.
- **Up to five Quiz or three Collect opinions/Q&A slides:** Create up to 5 Quiz slides or 3 Poll-type slides.
- **AI features:** Use free AI assistance to generate engaging slides tailored to your needs.
- **Collaborative editing:** Collaborate with friends and colleagues by allowing them to edit the presentation with you.
- **PowerPoint Integrations:** Insert interactive AhaSlides slides directly into your existing PowerPoint presentations.
- **PowerPoint/PDF import:** Import your existing PPT, PPTX, and PDF files.
If your presentation has more than 5 Quiz or 3 Poll-type slides, you'll only be able to host an event with up to 3 participants.

If you need to host larger events or use more quiz or question slides, consider checking out our upgraded plans for more flexibility: [Pricing page](https://ahaslides.com/pricing/)

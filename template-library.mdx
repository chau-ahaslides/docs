The AhaSlides Template Library is a collection of ready-made, fully interactive presentations you can grab and use in minutes. Instead of building slides from scratch, you pick a template that fits your use case, download it to your library, and tailor it to your audience. It's the fastest way to run a polished quiz, poll, icebreaker, or training session without starting from a blank canvas.

## Template categories

The library covers a wide range of use cases across different presentation types:

## Browsing the template library

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Templates section">
    Log in to your AhaSlides account and click **Templates** in the left-hand sidebar. The full library loads with all available templates displayed as cards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search">
    Scroll through the library to explore what's available, or type keywords into the search bar at the top to narrow results. For example, try searching *icebreaker*, *quiz*, or *feedback* to find templates relevant to your session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview a template">
    Click any template card to see a slide-by-slide summary of its contents. The preview shows you the slide types, questions, and layout before you commit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading and using a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the template you want">
    From the preview, click **Get Template**. AhaSlides copies the template into your **My Presentations** library and opens it automatically in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset the response data">
    Every downloaded template may include sample response data from previous sessions. Clear this before your session so your results start fresh.

    <Warning>
      Always reset the results before presenting a downloaded template. Old response data will appear on screen as if your audience already answered. See the [guide to resetting results](/features/reports#resetting-results) for exact steps.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise the content">
    Edit the template to match your topic, audience, and brand. See [Customising a template](#customising-a-template) below for what you can change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Present or share">
    When you're ready, click **Present** to launch your session live, or use the **Share** options to send the join link or QR code to your audience in advance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customising a template

Once a template is in your library, it behaves exactly like any presentation you built from scratch. You can change any part of it:

<Accordion title="Edit questions and answers">
  Click any interactive slide in the editor to update the question text, answer options, correct answers, and point values. You're not locked into the template's original content.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Add or remove slides">
  Use the slide panel on the left to reorder, duplicate, or delete slides. Add new interactive slides — polls, word clouds, Q&A, and more — anywhere in the deck.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Change the theme and colours">
  Open the **Theme** settings to swap the background, font, and colour palette so the presentation matches your brand or event style.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Upload your own images and media">
  Replace placeholder images on any slide with your own photos, logos, or graphics by clicking the image area and uploading a file.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Adjust timers and settings">
  On each quiz slide, you can change the countdown timer length, toggle whether to show correct answers, and adjust scoring. On poll slides, you can switch between single and multiple-choice responses.
</Accordion>

## Tips for getting the most out of templates

<Tip>
  The Template Library grows regularly. Check back before each session — you might find a template that saves you an hour of building time.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Downloaded templates are full copies saved to your library. Customising one never affects the original template, so you can re-download a fresh copy whenever you need a clean version.
</Tip>

The Template Library gives you a head start with professionally built interactive presentations. Whether you are preparing a quiz night, a team icebreaker, or a training session, you can browse by category, preview any template to see exactly how it is structured, and download a copy to customise for your own session — all without starting from a blank slide.

## Browsing & Previewing Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Template Library">
    Click **Templates** in the left sidebar of your Presentations Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search">
    Browse by category using the menu, or type a keyword into the search bar to find templates on a specific topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a template preview">
    Click any template card to open its preview page, where you can see details about the presentation including the number of slides and the interactive types used.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll through the slides">
    Use the slide panel in the preview to scroll through all the slides, check the questions, and confirm the template fits your needs before downloading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading and Using a Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Get Template">
    On the template preview page, click the **Get Template** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AhaSlides copies the template">
    AhaSlides automatically creates a full copy of the template in your **My Presentations** library.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the results reset">
    A warning appears noting that any pre-existing results data will be reset. Click **Confirm** to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise and present">
    Your copy opens in the slide editor. Edit the content, adjust the settings, and present whenever you are ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customising Your Template

Once you have downloaded a template, every part of it is yours to edit. Use the accordion below to find guidance on common customisation tasks.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Edit questions and answer options">
    Click any slide in the slide editor to select it. The question text and all answer options become editable directly on the slide canvas. Click any field and type to make your changes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Add or remove slides">
    Use the slide panel on the left side of the editor to manage your slides. Right-click any slide thumbnail for options to duplicate or delete it, or click the **+** button at the bottom of the panel to add a new slide.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Change theme and colours">
    Open the **Style** panel using the paintbrush icon in the top toolbar. From there, choose a preset theme, customise individual colours, change fonts, and upload a background image to match your branding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Upload images and media">
    Click the image placeholder on any slide to open the media picker. Upload an image from your device, paste a URL, or search the built-in image library to find the right visual for your slide.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Set timers and other slide settings">
    Open the slide settings panel on the right side of the editor. From here you can set a countdown timer, toggle whether the correct answer is revealed after voting, adjust participant limits, and configure other per-slide options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  The template library is updated regularly with new content. Check back before your next session to see if a new template fits your needs better than building from scratch.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Downloaded templates are full independent copies stored in your My Presentations library. Any edits you make to your copy never affect the original template in the library, so other users always see the unmodified version.
</Note>

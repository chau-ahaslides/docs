You can set up your quiz to be played in teams. You can also choose the scoring rules and team names.

> **Note:** Please note that this feature is only available on **Edu Medium, Edu Large, Pro, and Enterprise plans**

## How Does it Work?

When teamplay is enabled, players will join other players in teams to compete for points against other teams.

Before presenting your quiz to your players, you will need to set up the teams and the scoring rules. When a player joins your quiz, they will be asked to select one of the teams you have created.

## How to Set Up your Teams

1. On the presentation editor, go to ***Settings*** and then **General quiz settings**.

2. Click switch next to *'***Play as team**, and then the the blue text labelled **Set up.**

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn42f82da8fd93cbae849c54e4b1589f2f2decd82527544ba9920147d61e6bc441095be38999cd4fd485887d2d732337bf?inline=true)

The Team set up menu will appear

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnac010dd5ebf6126ac3eb634cf8d025f6f76627c9e65270450a0daa0492f4e3e2e1532b685cd7ee92d133a0d104201fa9?inline=true)

3. Set the number of teams that will be playing in your quiz and the maximum number of players allowed in each team. If you don't know the number of teams or players per team while setting up, you can estimate a high number so as to be sure that everyone can join the team they want.

- Maximum number of teams: 500
- Maximum team size: 1200

4. Choose how your teams will score points. There are 3 options to choose from...

i. **Average score of all members** - Including zero points for those who answer incorrectly or do not answer.
ii. **Total score of all members** - Note that this rule will handicap teams with fewer members.
iii. **Fastest answer only** - Only register the score of the fastest answer from each team (even if it's incorrect).

5. Change the team names. If you plan to allow your players to come up with team names, they will have to inform you of their name so you can write it in here.

6. If you would like to hide the individual ranking on the players' screens, you can enable the option **Hide individual leaderboard.** The presenter will still be able to change between team and individual leaderboards.

## How your Participants Join Teams

When you present your quiz, your audience will be asked to enter their name, choose an avatar and select one of the teams you have set up...

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn5bfed31c49c08597c45b238ad138e6295b2409d52f55b1a096bc40f3266253473d0c103612f57ad6a4b40f96e70906c5?inline=true)

They can also change team by clicking the **(change team)** text if they are still at the lobby screen

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnfdfd3b26e1475b62c75d664b5d05b528807b8f26b5a182753455203df90bf1e3e1f15d5562d6190eee4b432ae6e33b85?inline=true)

On the presenter's screen, you will be able to see which players have joined which teams in the quiz lobby...

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn7f3a332a615f699a23718fe6f45633ace0d12431eef6513aa0cf9312bdc12e8b35db3bc50497605e8e71b4c37c4f3fad?inline=true)

> **Note:** You can also add a new team from this quiz lobby by clicking the **+ Add a team** button in the centre. This will add a new team that players can immediately join, though you will not be able to edit the name of this team.

When you reach a leaderboard in your quiz, you will see the scores of each team. You can also click **Show Individual Ranking** to reveal how each player in each team is performing.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn8d32eb3b1dd676b7161213eff42a3610de8a2f9049a1996f2c0c31811843301c3524bab06b8283f0bca77952ee54887f?inline=true)

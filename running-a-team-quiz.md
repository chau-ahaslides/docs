You can set up your quiz to be played in teams. You can also choose the scoring rules and team names.

Please note that this feature is only available on **Edu Medium, Edu Large, Pro, and Enterprise plans**


## How Does it Work?

When teamplay is enabled, players will join other players in teams to compete for points against other teams.


## How to Set Up your Teams

- On the presentation editor, go to **Settings** and then **General quiz settings**.
- Click switch next to '**Play as team**', and then the blue text labelled **Set up**.
- Set the number of teams that will be playing in your quiz and the maximum number of players allowed in each team.
- Choose how your teams will score points. There are 3 options: **Average score of all members**, **Total score of all members**, **Fastest answer only**.
- Change the team names.
- If you would like to hide the individual ranking on the players' screens, you can enable the option **Hide individual leaderboard**.

## How your Participants Join Teams

When you present your quiz, your audience will be asked to enter their name, choose an avatar and select one of the teams you have set up.

Both tools make presentations interactive. This page breaks down where they differ — on AI, activity types, free plan limits, and pricing — so you can make an informed choice.

## 1. Activity types

Mentimeter covers the core interaction types well. AhaSlides goes broader — it's a single platform for every engagement format you'd need across presentations, training, and events.

**AhaSlides**

- Quiz — multiple types: categories, correct order, match pairs, type answer
- Poll — multiple choice, image poll, rating scale
- Word cloud
- Open-ended / brainstorm
- Q&A with moderation
- Survey
- Spinner wheel
- Per-student grade and response reports

**Mentimeter**

- Quiz — single correct answer, exact-match grading only
- Poll — multiple choice, ranking, rating
- Word cloud
- Open-ended
- Q&A
- No Spinner Wheel
- No built-in survey flow
- No per-student grade reports

Two gaps G2 reviewers flag consistently: Mentimeter doesn't support multiple correct answers in quizzes, and exact-match grading penalises students for a typo or rounding difference.

> "It would be better if there were more features designed to encourage conversations either before or after using the app. The quiz functionality feels limited."
>
> — Dr. Emily C., Lecturer, Higher Education (G2 review of Mentimeter)

## 3. Free plan

Both free plans cap sessions at 50 participants. The difference is how the cap works.

Mentimeter counts participants across the entire month — 50 total. A teacher running two classes of 30 students hits the limit after the first session. AhaSlides resets the count every session, with unlimited sessions per month.

| Feature              | AhaSlides                  | Mentimeter                  |
|----------------------|----------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Participant limit    | 50 per session             | 50 per month total          |
| Sessions per month   | Unlimited                  | Capped by monthly limit     |
| AI features          | Included                   | Menti AI included           |
| Quiz slides          | Up to 5 per presentation   | Limited                     |
| Response export      | —                          | Paid only                   |

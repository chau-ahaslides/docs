You can embed any AhaSlides presentation directly on a webpage using a standard HTML iFrame. This lets your website visitors participate in polls, quizzes, and word clouds without navigating to a separate URL. All you need is your presentation's audience link and a few lines of HTML.

<Warning>
  Website embedding is **not officially supported** by AhaSlides. You may encounter unexpected bugs or display issues. Use this approach for low-stakes or experimental use cases, and test thoroughly before relying on it in a live environment.
</Warning>

## The iFrame Code Snippet

Paste the following code into your webpage's HTML where you want the presentation to appear. Replace the `src` URL with your own presentation's audience link.

```html
<iframe
  src="https://audience.ahaslides.com/your-presentation-code"
  width="100%"
  height="800px">
  <p>Your browser does not support iframes.</p>
</iframe>
```

Your audience link follows the format `https://audience.ahaslides.com/` followed by your unique presentation code. Find this link by opening your presentation in AhaSlides and clicking **Present** — the URL shown in the audience view is the one to use.

## Customising Width and Height

Adjust the `width` and `height` attributes to fit your page layout:

```html
<!-- Full-width, fixed height — good for landing pages -->
<iframe
  src="https://audience.ahaslides.com/your-presentation-code"
  width="100%"
  height="800px">
  <p>Your browser does not support iframes.</p>
</iframe>

<!-- Fixed dimensions — good for sidebars or cards -->
<iframe
  src="https://audience.ahaslides.com/your-presentation-code"
  width="640px"
  height="480px">
  <p>Your browser does not support iframes.</p>
</iframe>
```

<Note>
  Setting `width="100%"` makes the embed responsive to the width of its parent container. This is the recommended approach for most layouts. Set a fixed pixel height that comfortably displays the interactive content — `800px` works well for full-page embeds.
</Note>

## Tips for a Good Embed Experience

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Start your presentation first" icon="play">
    Make sure your presentation is live (i.e. you have clicked **Present** in AhaSlides) before visitors arrive on the page. The iFrame shows the audience view, so the session must be active.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Test across browsers" icon="browser">
    Some browsers or browser extensions block iFrames from third-party domains. Test your embed in Chrome, Firefox, and Safari before publishing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consider mobile viewports" icon="mobile">
    On narrow screens, a fixed-width iFrame may be clipped. Use `width="100%"` and ensure your page's CSS does not constrain the container too tightly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keep sessions short" icon="clock">
    Embedded sessions work best for focused, time-limited activities like a single poll or a short quiz rather than lengthy multi-slide presentations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

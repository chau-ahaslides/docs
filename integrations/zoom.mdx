The AhaSlides Zoom app lets you run interactive presentations from directly inside a Zoom meeting or webinar. Participants respond without leaving Zoom — and you don't need to share your screen to show slides.

<Warning>
  Both presenter and participants must use the **Zoom Workplace desktop app** or **Zoom Webinar desktop app**. Mobile is not supported.
</Warning>

## Installing the AhaSlides App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Zoom account">
    Go to [zoom.us](https://zoom.us) and sign in with your Zoom credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the App Marketplace">
    Open the **Apps** menu in the top navigation and select **App Marketplace**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for AhaSlides">
    Type **AhaSlides** in the search bar and select it from the results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add">
    Click **Add** on the AhaSlides app page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorise the app">
    Review and accept the permissions requested, then click **Authorise** to complete the installation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The app is now available in your Zoom meetings.

## Hosting a Presentation in Zoom

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start or join a Zoom meeting">
    Launch a scheduled meeting or start an instant meeting from the Zoom desktop app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Apps panel">
    Click the **Apps** button in the meeting toolbar at the bottom of the meeting window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open AhaSlides">
    Find and open AhaSlides from your installed apps list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Host a presentation'">
    Click **Host a presentation** to enter presenter mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a presentation">
    Select a presentation from your library to load it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable screen sharing when prompted">
    Allow Zoom to share your screen so participants can see your slides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite participants">
    Share the access code or link displayed in your presentation with your audience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Present">
    Use the AhaSlides controls to navigate slides and manage live interactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Participants Join

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Invite Notification">
    Participants receive an automatic invite notification in the Zoom meeting chat. Clicking the notification opens the AhaSlides panel directly inside their Zoom window — no extra steps required.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Zoom Apps Panel">
    Participants who have the AhaSlides Zoom app installed can open it directly from their own **Apps** button in the meeting toolbar.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Access Code">
    Participants visit [audience.ahaslides.com](https://audience.ahaslides.com) and enter the access code displayed in your presentation. This works even if they don't have the Zoom app installed.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Uninstalling the App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Zoom App Marketplace">
    Navigate to [zoom.us](https://zoom.us) and open the **App Marketplace**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your managed apps">
    Click **Manage** to view all apps currently added to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find AhaSlides">
    Locate AhaSlides in your list of added apps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Remove">
    Click **Remove** to uninstall the app from your Zoom account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do participants need to install the AhaSlides Zoom app?">
    No. Only the presenter needs the app. Participants can respond via the access code at [audience.ahaslides.com](https://audience.ahaslides.com).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do participants need an AhaSlides account?">
    No. Participants do not need an AhaSlides account to join and respond.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use AhaSlides in Zoom without the Marketplace app?">
    Yes. Share your screen and present your AhaSlides deck as usual. Participants can join via the audience URL or QR code displayed on screen.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does this work on the Zoom mobile app?">
    No. The AhaSlides Zoom integration is supported on the Zoom desktop app only.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

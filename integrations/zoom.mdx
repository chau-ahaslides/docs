The AhaSlides app for Zoom lets you run interactive presentations without ever leaving your meeting. Once installed, participants receive an in-Zoom invitation to open the AhaSlides panel on their own screen and respond in real time — no separate browser tabs, no access codes to announce.

## Requirements

<Warning>
  Both the presenter and participants must use the **Zoom Workplace or Zoom Webinar desktop app** on a PC or laptop. The Zoom mobile app is **not supported** for this integration. Participants on mobile should join via the access code in a browser instead.
</Warning>

- Zoom Workplace or Zoom Webinar desktop app (PC or laptop)
- An AhaSlides account for the host (presenter) — participants do not need one

## Installing the AhaSlides App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Zoom">
    Open the Zoom Workplace or Webinar desktop app and sign in with your Zoom account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the App Marketplace">
    Click **Apps** in the Zoom toolbar, then select **Add apps**. Search for **AhaSlides** in the Zoom App Marketplace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the app">
    Click **Add** on the AhaSlides listing, then select **Allow** to grant the necessary permissions. AhaSlides now appears in your **My Apps** section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Hosting a Presentation in a Zoom Meeting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AhaSlides in Zoom">
    Start or join a Zoom meeting, then open AhaSlides from your **Zoom Apps** panel. Alternatively, launch AhaSlides from **My Apps** before the meeting begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Host a presentation'">
    Click **Host a presentation** and sign in to your AhaSlides account when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a presentation">
    Browse your presentation library or open a folder, then select the deck you want to present.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable screen sharing">
    Select **Allow** when Zoom asks for screen-sharing permission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite participants">
    Select **Allow** to send an invitation notification to all meeting participants. They can then open the AhaSlides panel directly in Zoom.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Present as usual">
    Run your AhaSlides presentation normally. Participant responses appear in the panel in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can re-invite participants at any time by clicking the **arrow icon** at the top of the AhaSlides app panel inside the meeting.
</Tip>

## How Participants Join

Participants have three ways to open the AhaSlides panel during the meeting:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="From an invite notification" icon="bell">
    Click **Open** in the invitation notification that appears when the host starts the presentation. This is the fastest path — no extra steps required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatically via Zoom Apps" icon="bolt">
    Click **Apps** in the Zoom meeting toolbar, find AhaSlides, and add it. Once added, the panel connects automatically whenever the host starts a presentation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="By entering an access code" icon="keyboard">
    Click **Apps**, add the AhaSlides app, select **Join as a Participant**, and type in the access code provided by the host.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Uninstalling the App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Zoom App Marketplace">
    Log in at [marketplace.zoom.us](https://marketplace.zoom.us).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your added apps">
    Click **Manage**, then select **Added apps**. Search for **AhaSlides** in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the app">
    Click the three-dot menu next to the AhaSlides app and select **Remove**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Do participants need to install the AhaSlides app separately?">
  No. Participants can accept the host's invitation directly from the Zoom notification, or open AhaSlides from their own Zoom Apps panel. No separate installation or AhaSlides account is required.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do participants need an AhaSlides account?">
  No. Only the host (presenter) requires an AhaSlides account. Participants join as guests using a display name.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use AhaSlides in Zoom without the Marketplace app?">
  Yes. Open your presentation in a browser, go live, then use Zoom's built-in screen sharing to present. Participants join via the access code as usual. This approach works well but does not give participants the integrated in-Zoom panel experience.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does this work on the Zoom mobile app?">
  No. The AhaSlides Zoom integration is only supported on the Zoom Workplace or Webinar **desktop app** on PC or laptop. Participants on mobile should join via the access code in their mobile browser instead.
</Accordion>

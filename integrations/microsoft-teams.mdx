The AhaSlides add-in brings your interactive presentations directly into Microsoft Teams. Participants respond from their own devices while you present — no separate links, no access codes to read out, and no app-switching required. Everything happens inside the Teams window.

## Installing the Add-in

You can add AhaSlides to Teams from either a channel tab or the meeting toolbar.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From a channel or chat tab">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open a channel or group chat">
        Navigate to any Teams channel or group chat where you want AhaSlides available.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add a new tab">
        Click the **+** icon at the top of the channel to open the tab picker.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find AhaSlides">
        Search for **AhaSlides** and select it from the results.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in">
        Sign in with your AhaSlides account to connect the tab to your presentation library.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From the meeting toolbar">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Join or start a meeting">
        Open any Teams meeting. The AhaSlides button appears directly in the meeting toolbar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open AhaSlides">
        Click **AhaSlides** in the toolbar. The panel opens on the right side of the Teams window and displays your presentation library.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Can't see the button?">
        Click **Apps** in the meeting toolbar and add AhaSlides from there. It will appear in the toolbar for all future meetings.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Presenting in a Meeting

Once the panel is open, your full presentation library is available — including individual presentations and folders, each showing slide count and recent participant stats.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse your library">
    Scroll through your presentations or open a folder to find the right deck. Each item shows the number of slides and recent participant activity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share a presentation">
    Click **Share** on any presentation to go live. Your slides appear in the main meeting view, visible to all participants immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate slides">
    Use the **← →** arrows or click any thumbnail in the slide strip at the bottom to move between slides. The current position (e.g. **4 / 10**) is shown in the centre.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View presenter notes">
    The **Notes** panel sits to the right of the slide. Click it to read or edit speaker notes for the current slide — only you can see these.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch presentations">
    The AhaSlides panel stays open alongside your presentation. Browse your library and click **Share** on a different deck at any time without leaving the meeting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Participants See

Participants open the AhaSlides panel on their own devices automatically — no join code is needed when they are already in the Teams meeting. The participant screen is organised into four tabs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Slides" icon="display">
    The current interactive slide — input fields, answer choices, and voting buttons update in real time as the presenter advances.
  </Card>
  <Card title="List" icon="list">
    A scrollable overview of every slide in the deck so participants can see what's coming up.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Q&A" icon="message-question">
    Audience questions sortable by **Top**, **New**, **Answered**, or **My questions**. Participants can upvote each other's submissions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="user">
    The participant's display name and personal settings for the session.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Responding to Slides

Participants interact directly from the panel without switching apps:

<Accordion title="Interaction types">
  - **Word Cloud** — type up to 3 words and submit; words appear instantly on the presenter's cloud
  - **Poll** — select an answer; the bar chart updates live on the presenter view
  - **Open Ended** — type a response and optionally upload an image
  - **Rating Scale** — drag or tap to rate; results appear as a wavy line chart
  - **Brainstorm** — submit ideas, then vote on others' submissions in a second phase
  - **Quiz** — choose an answer before the timer runs out and get instant right/wrong feedback
  - **Q&A** — type and submit questions at any time, and upvote questions from other participants
</Accordion>

After answering, participants see a confirmation message and wait for the presenter to advance to the next slide.

### Emoji Reactions

Five emoji reactions — 👍 ❤️ 😂 😮 😠 — appear at the bottom of the participant panel throughout the entire session. Participants can react to any slide at any time, and reaction counts are visible to both presenter and participants. This is a useful signal for pacing and reading the room.

<Tip>
  The **Ask a question** button is always visible on the participant panel, regardless of which slide is active. Participants can submit Q&A questions at any point — not only on a dedicated Q&A slide.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Participants cannot see the content shared by the presenter">
  **Symptom:** The presenter shares a presentation, but one or more participants see an error message instead of the slides.

  **Cause:** This is a Microsoft Teams limitation that AhaSlides cannot control. It occurs when the participant is joining as a **guest** — that is, they do not have a Microsoft Teams account of their own.

  **Fix:** Ask the affected participant to sign in with a Microsoft Teams account and rejoin the meeting. If they don't have a Teams account, use the screen-sharing workaround instead: open your presentation in a browser, go live, then share your screen in Teams. Participants can join via the access code as usual.
</Accordion>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Do participants need to install the Teams add-in?">
  No. Participants see the AhaSlides panel automatically when you share a presentation. They only need to be present in the Teams meeting.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do participants need an AhaSlides account?">
  No. Participants join as guests by entering a display name. An AhaSlides account is only required to create and present.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use AhaSlides in Teams without the add-in?">
  Yes. Open your presentation in a browser, go live, then use Teams' built-in screen sharing to present. Participants join via the access code as usual. This works well but does not give participants the integrated panel experience inside Teams.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I switch presentations mid-meeting?">
  Yes. The AhaSlides panel stays open while you present. Browse your library and click **Share** on a different presentation at any time — no need to leave or restart the meeting.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is the add-in available on mobile Teams?">
  The presenter experience is optimised for the Teams desktop app. Participants can always respond from any device, including mobile.
</Accordion>

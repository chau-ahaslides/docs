AhaSlides is free to join and takes less than a minute to set up. You can sign up with a dedicated email and password, or use an existing Google or Microsoft account to skip the extra credentials entirely. If your organisation manages access centrally, single sign-on (SSO) is also supported. Choose the method that works best for you below.

<Note>
  All sign-up methods land you on the same free plan — no credit card required. See [what's included in the free plan](/billing/free-plan) before deciding whether to upgrade.
</Note>

## Sign-up methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password">
    Use any email address to create a standalone AhaSlides account.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
        Open [presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register) in your browser.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your details">
        Type your email address, choose a password, and enter your full name.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Submit">
        Click **Sign Up**. AhaSlides will create your account and take you straight to your presentations dashboard.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Google">
    Link your Google account to sign in without a separate password.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
        Open [presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Google">
        Click the **Sign up with Google** button.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select your account">
        Pick the Google account you want to link to AhaSlides and sign in when prompted.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Microsoft">
    Link your Microsoft account for one-click sign-in across Microsoft products.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
        Open [presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Microsoft">
        Click the **Sign up with Microsoft** button.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select your account">
        Pick the Microsoft account you want to link to AhaSlides and sign in when prompted.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="SSO">
    Single sign-on lets your team sign in through your organisation's identity provider — no separate AhaSlides passwords needed.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
        Open [presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/register).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose SSO">
        Click the **Sign up with SSO** button.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your work email">
        Type your organisation's email address and click **Log In**. AhaSlides will redirect you to your identity provider's login screen.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      SSO is available on Enterprise plans. To enable it for your team, contact the AhaSlides sales team at [ahaslides.com/enterprise](https://ahaslides.com/enterprise/).
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

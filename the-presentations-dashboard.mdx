The Presentations section is the first thing you see when you log in to AhaSlides, and it's where everything starts. From here you can spin up a new presentation, tidy your library into folders, take quick actions on individual slides, and search across your entire collection. This page walks you through every part of that workspace.

## The three views

In the left sidebar, click **Presentations** to expand it. You'll find three views underneath:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="My Presentations" icon="folder-open">
    All the presentations you own, including any folders you've created. This is your main working area.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shared with Me" icon="share-nodes">
    Presentations and folders that other AhaSlides users have shared with you. You can open and edit them (subject to permissions), but you can't use bulk actions here.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trash" icon="trash">
    Presentations you've sent to the trash. Restore them or permanently delete them from this view.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Creating a new presentation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Presentation">
    In the **My Presentations** view, click the **New Presentation** button near the top of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your presentation">
    Enter a name for your presentation. You can optionally set a custom access code at this point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start adding slides">
    AhaSlides takes you straight to the slide editor. Add interactive slides, content slides, and more.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Click the arrow next to the **New Presentation** button and choose **From a Template** to start from a ready-made design instead of a blank slate.
</Tip>

## Organising presentations into folders

Keep your library tidy by grouping related presentations into folders — and folders inside folders.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a folder">
    Click the **New Folder** button in the toolbar, give it a name, and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move presentations in">
    Select the presentations you want to move (see [Bulk actions](#bulk-actions) below), click **Move to**, and choose your folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create sub-folders if needed">
    Open any folder and click **New Folder** from inside it to create a sub-folder. You can nest folders up to 10 levels deep.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each folder can hold up to 200 presentations. If you need to create a new presentation inside a specific folder, open that folder first and then click **New Presentation** — it'll be placed there automatically.
</Note>

## Actions on a presentation

Hover over any presentation card to reveal quick-action buttons. For the full list, click the **⋮** menu on the right side of any row.

<Accordion title="Present">
  Launch the presentation in live-presenting mode immediately. This button also appears inline when you hover a row so you can start in a single click.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Rename">
  Opens an inline field so you can rename the presentation without leaving the dashboard.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="View Report">
  Opens the response data report for that presentation. This option requires at least one completed live session.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Reset Results">
  Permanently clears all response data collected for that presentation. This action cannot be undone.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Duplicate">
  Creates an identical copy in your current folder. Useful for reusing a presentation structure across different events.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Share">
  Opens the sharing dialog where you can invite collaborators by email or generate a shareable link.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Move to">
  Moves the presentation to a different folder in your library.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Move to Trash">
  Sends the presentation to the Trash. Restore it from there within 30 days or delete it permanently.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Leave">
  Removes your own access to a presentation that was shared with you. Only appears on shared presentations.
</Accordion>

## Actions on a folder

Click the **⋮** menu on any folder row to manage the folder itself:

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Rename** | Renames the folder. |
| **Share** | Invites collaborators to the entire folder (available on team plans). |
| **Delete** | Permanently deletes the folder and every presentation inside it. |
| **Leave** | Removes your access to a shared folder. |

<Warning>
  Deleting a folder permanently deletes all presentations inside it. There is no way to recover them. Move any presentations you want to keep before deleting a folder.
</Warning>

## Bulk actions

To act on multiple presentations at once:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select presentations">
    Hover over any presentation row and click the checkbox that appears. A floating action bar appears at the bottom of the screen as soon as at least one item is ticked. Continue selecting as many presentations as you need, or click **Select all** in the action bar to grab everything in the current view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an action">
    From the floating action bar, choose:
    - **Move to** — moves all selected presentations to a folder you choose.
    - **Move to trash** — sends all selected presentations to the Trash at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel if needed">
    Click **✕** in the action bar to deselect everything and dismiss the bar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Bulk actions are not available in the **Shared with Me** view.
</Note>

## Sorting and searching

- **Sort** — click the sort control at the top of the list to order your presentations by *Recently edited*, *Recently created*, *Alphabetical*, or their reverse equivalents.
- **Search** — use the search bar in the top header to find any presentation by name across all your folders at once.

## Importing a presentation

Bring an existing file from outside AhaSlides — for example, a PowerPoint deck — straight into your library:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Import">
    Click the **Import** button in the dashboard toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your file">
    Select your PowerPoint (PPTX), PPT, or PDF file from your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the result">
    AhaSlides converts the file into an editable presentation and opens it in the editor, ready for you to add interactive slides.
  </Step>
</Steps>

# How to Change and Manage Fonts

You can change the font used throughout your AhaSlides presentation and arrange your favourite fonts for easy, immediate access.

> **Note:** Please note that this feature is only available on **Pro and Enterprise plans.**

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnb826db4bb34dbfb79e87139d0d1f81dc7e58cc322e46da22c4c4f2cc74f64159616503c8942befda2a4f0571b1041a9e?inline=true)

## How to Change Font

In the AhaSlides editor, select any of the slides in your presentation.

Head to the right-column, select the '**Design**' tab and scroll down to '**Text**'.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn511d96f884da793ac25085adc0edf81276adeda57dd232279123171fa8066548d236491948408861de23ac14a0c4c01b?inline=true)

Use the dropdown menu to select your desired font.

> **Note:** This will change the font across your entire presentation. It will also be visible on your audience's screens.

## How to Manage your Fonts

Click on the dropdown menu under '**Text**' in the '**Design**' tab of a slide. Then, click the '**More fonts**' option at the top.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn9c94b4b0985e02ba4db3b6747e67b32c61b63363099e826e75fb0041dc80d4e1163179b8a9421c7d0134b1f280dd5d9b?inline=true)

This will open the font library. Here, you can select from a larger bank of 250 fonts to build your own selection of preferred fonts.

For any fonts that you would like to be able to use, **check the checkbox** next to the font name. This will add the font to the **My fonts** column on the right.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn76461f0969045b2666362213739e97a456e490c937ff8386b419af3ea82c4fb847b51c0471feb8df777b19fc84c54ffb?inline=true)

Once you've finished selecting your fonts, click **Done**.

Those fonts you selected will now be available in the '**Text**' dropdown menu.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnca4273d095c737c08081f46db8f3811cccfb1907959cd0c166e8e06b7f5950777c76c597a1b6b0310081954133689633?inline=true)

> **Note:** Once you have selected your fonts from the font library, those fonts will be your immediate options across **all presentations in your account**.

## How Fonts Work with the Content Slide

The content slide has its own small font library, which is displayed in the top left corner of the editor when editing text.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsne9ba7e7426fa37e96e46b6ac18d1454a89a9d2e3b4a95ea22580502b1d33440c4ddd8d28bb2ec81c13ac6fde2f51253c?inline=true)

However, it is still possible to change to a font in your font library by selecting the '**Design**' tab and finding your preferred font in the '**Text**' dropdown menu.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn49ea86ce6c684d1995a61c6822892f6f4f3223a0e05b94822b14e70644fb4cd926dcff15106a363290077ba1f6630464?inline=true)

> **Note:** Please note that it is currently **not possible to import fonts** to AhaSlides.

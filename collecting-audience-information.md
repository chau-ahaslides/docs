Collect audience information like names, email addresses and names of organisations from your participants before they join your presentation or while they answer an Open-Ended or Brainstorm slide.

Please note that while this feature is available on all paid plans, only **certain paid plans** can see collected participant information in the Excel export after the presentation is over.


## How to Collect Audience Info


### 1. In the Setting menu:

- Head to the **Settings** menu and click on **Collect audience info**.
- Check the box labelled **Ask participants for info before they join**.
- Start by writing the heading to your form, then click the button next to **Add fields** to select information fields to add.

### 2. In an Open-Ended or Brainstorm slide

- When editing an Open-Ended or Brainstorm slide, click the button next to **Collect audience info**.
- Click on the field that you want to add.

## Adding information fields

You can add up to 8 fields, including: 3 default fields (Name, Email, Avatar) and 5 custom fields.


## Seeing Collected Info in the Excel Export

Once participants have entered their info, it will be stored in the Excel file that contains your full presentation report. Download the Excel file and click on the second sheet labelled 'Participants'.

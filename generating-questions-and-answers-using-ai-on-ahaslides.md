# Generating Questions and Answers using AI on AhaSlides

AhaSlides now offers AI-powered answer generation for quizzes and poll helping you quickly create questions and answer options.

> For a complete guide to creating and running quizzes on AhaSlides, see our article: [How to Make and Run a Quiz](how-to-make-and-run-a-quiz.md).

## How to Generate Questions Using AI

1. **Create a Presentation**
   Start by creating a presentation and giving it a name.
2. **Add a Quiz slide**
   Create any quiz slide within your presentation.
3. **Select a Generated Question**
   Under 'Your Question,' AI-generated questions will appear based on your presentation name. Simply choose the question you'd like to ask your audience.

> Currently, AI question generation is available on all quiz slides.

## How to Generate Answers Using AI

1. **Create a Quiz or Poll slide**
   Start by creating any quiz or Poll slide within your presentation.
2. **Type Your Question**
   In the 'Content' tab, type the question you'd like to ask your audience.
3. **Generate Answer Options**
   Click the **Generate options** button. This will activate the AI to produce suitable answer choices for your question.

> AI answer generation is available on all quiz and poll slides.

This feature uses AI services from OpenAI and Groq, and we do not send any personal information to these providers. For more details, please review [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy) and [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/).

The Q&A feature lets you gather questions from your audience. You can then organise all these questions and answer them all one-by-one.


## How Does Q&A Work?

On AhaSlides, 'Q&A' refers your audience sending you questions during a presentation, you seeing those questions and then answering them vocally.

There are **2 ways** in which you can hold a Q&A with your audience...


### 1. The Q&A Slide

If you want questions from your audience to come at a certain time in your presentation, you can set up a Q&A slide to gather those questions and address them one-by-one. Participants will only be able to send you Q&A questions while you're presenting this slide.


### 2. On all Slides

This function is only available on **Essential, Pro, Enterprise** and **Edu Large** plans.

If you want your audience to send you questions whenever they think of them, not just on a dedicated Q&A slide, you can arrange that in your presentation settings.


## How to Arrange Q&A Questions

When you open up the Q&A interface, you will see any unanswered questions in the centre and a column of **5 filters** on the left: **Top questions**, **Pinned**, **Newest**, **Oldest**, and **Answered**.


## Q&A Settings

- **Filter profanity**
- **Moderate audience's questions** (only available on Pro, Enterprise and Edu Large plans) - approve and dismiss questions from your audience before they are submitted.
- **Allow audience to see each other's questions**
- **Allow anonymous questions**
Q&A slide supports up to 1,000 questions per presentation.

# Sharing a Copy of your Presentation with Other AhaSlides Users

With AhaSlides, you can share your presentation with other users, and they can copy that presentation to their accounts.

In order to do this, head to the presentation editor. In the **Share** tab in the top bar, choose **Share Slides**. You will find a **Copy link** button

Copy that link and share it wherever you want. Anyone who copies and pastes it into their address bar will be able to view your presentation.

You can also grant permission to other users for your presentation, including:

- **Include Slide Notes:** Receivers can view your notes when they copy this presentation to their account.
- **Allow Comments:** Anyone with the link can leave comments on the shared presentation.

To enable these, click the 3 **dots** button next to the **Copy link** button. These options will appear.

While viewing your presentation, AhaSlides users who are logged in will see an option to copy it to their account. If they are not users or aren't logged in, they will see options to sign up or log in to their account.

If someone copies a presentation to their account, **the response data will not be copied from the original presentation**.

The new user of the presentation will be able to edit that presentation in any way that they wish.

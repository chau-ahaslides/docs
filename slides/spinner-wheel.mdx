The Spinner Wheel is a slide that lets you pick any entry at random by spinning a wheel on screen. Fill the wheel with names, options, or tasks, then press the button at the centre — the wheel spins and slows to land on one entry. Both you and your audience see the result at the same time. It's a simple, high-energy way to make random selections feel fair and fun.

<Note>
  Only the **presenter** can spin the wheel. Spin results are not saved in the Excel export file. The Spinner Wheel supports up to **5,000 entries**.
</Note>

## Two ways to add entries

### Add entries manually

Type entries directly into the **Entries** field in the right panel of the editor. Press **Enter** after each entry to add the next one, or paste a list from your clipboard all at once. Click **Save** when you're done.

<Tip>
  You can also add or edit entries while presenting in live mode — no need to return to the editor if you think of more entries mid-session.
</Tip>

### Autofill with participant names

If you want to pick a random participant, check **Participant name autofill** in the right panel. The wheel fills itself automatically with every participant's name as they join by scanning the QR code and entering their name — no manual typing needed.

You can also switch this on while presenting. If participants already joined a quiz earlier in the same presentation, their names carry over automatically — they won't need to re-enter them.

## Remove entries

To delete a manual entry, hover over it in the entry list on the right panel and click the **bin icon** labelled *Delete this entry*.

## Settings

<Accordion title="Spin time">
  Sets how long the wheel spins before landing on an entry. Increase it for maximum suspense — you can set up to **1,200 seconds**. The default is a few seconds, which is enough for most sessions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Duplicate entries">
  Multiply the existing entries in the wheel by up to **10 times**. This gives entries with higher frequency a greater chance of being selected — useful for weighted random draws. Uncheck the box to remove the duplicates.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Filter profanity">
  Hides inappropriate words submitted by participants across all slides in your presentation (English only).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Hide entry board">
  Removes the list of entries from the wheel display during a live presentation, so participants only see the wheel itself — not the full list of what's on it.
</Accordion>

## Spin the wheel

With your entries loaded, press the button at the centre of the wheel to spin it. The selected entry is displayed on both the presenter screen and participants' devices when the wheel stops.

## Tips for getting the most out of the Spinner Wheel

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Word Cloud → Spinner Wheel workflow" icon="shuffle">
    Create a Word Cloud slide first and ask participants to submit entries. Then change the slide type from Word Cloud to Spinner Wheel — every submission automatically becomes a wheel entry. This lets your audience populate the wheel themselves.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use autofill for fair draws" icon="users">
    Enable **Participant name autofill** when you want to pick a random winner or volunteer. Every participant who joins is automatically included, so the draw is transparent and fair.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add suspense with a longer spin time" icon="clock">
    Increase the spin time to 10–20 seconds for high-stakes picks. The gradual slowdown builds anticipation that a quick spin doesn't.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paste entries in bulk" icon="clipboard">
    If you have a long list in a spreadsheet or document, copy the whole column and paste it into the Entries field — AhaSlides splits it into individual entries automatically.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

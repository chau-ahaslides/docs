AhaSlides gives you a full toolkit of interactive and display slides. Use this page to see every slide type at a glance, then open any card for its dedicated setup guide.

## Interactive Slides

Collect live input from your audience — votes, words, ideas, questions, and more.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Poll" icon="chart-bar" href="/slides/poll">
    Audience votes on preset options, shown live as a bar, donut, or pie chart.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Word Cloud" icon="cloud" href="/slides/word-cloud">
    Submitted words grow larger as more people repeat them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open Ended" icon="comment-dots" href="/slides/open-ended">
    Free-text responses collected on a live wall.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Q&A" icon="circle-question" href="/slides/qa">
    Audience submits and upvotes questions at any point.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Brainstorm" icon="brain" href="/slides/brainstorm">
    Three-stage ideation: submit, vote, then reveal ranked results.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Idea Board" icon="rectangle-list" href="/slides/idea-board">
    Collaborative card board with AI grouping and a voting stage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rating Scale" icon="star-half-stroke" href="/slides/rating-scale">
    Rate multiple statements on a numeric scale you define.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ranking" icon="arrow-up-wide-short" href="/slides/ranking">
    Order options by preference, scored with the Borda count method.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pin on Image" icon="map-pin" href="/slides/pin-on-image">
    Drop pins on a custom image, shown as a live heat map.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spinner Wheel" icon="rotate" href="/slides/spinner-wheel">
    Randomiser wheel that picks entries or participant names.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

  <Card title="Type Answer" icon="keyboard" href="/slides/type-answer">

  <Card title="Match Pairs" icon="link" href="/slides/match-pairs">

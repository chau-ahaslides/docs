You can change or reset your password for security reasons or reset it if you forget it.


## Change your password

- Go to **My Profile**.
- Scroll down to **Change password**
- Enter your **Current Password** and **New password**
- Click **Update password**
That's it. You've successfully updated your password!


## Reset your password

- Go to this link: [https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/forgot-password](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/pages/forgot-password)
- Type in your email address using **only lowercase letters**. Capital letters might prevent the system from recognizing your address.
- Click **Reset Password**
- You can follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

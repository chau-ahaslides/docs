You can hide the logo in the top-right of your slides or change it to a logo/image of your choice.

Please note that this feature is only available on **Pro and Enterprise plans.**


## What is the Logo?

The logo sits in the top-right corner of all of the slides in your presentation. Your audience will see it on their phones as well as the presenter's screen.

By default, the logo will be the AhaSlides logo, but users on a Pro plan can hide it or change it to their own logo. Doing this once on any slide will take effect across your whole presentation.


## How to Hide the Logo

Hiding the logo means that you will have **no logo** showing in the top-right of your slides.

- Navigate to the AhaSlides editor and hover over the logo in the top-right of the presentation canvas.
- Click the logo and select **'Hide'**.
- To reveal the logo once again, hover over the space in the top-right corner and click **'Unhide'**.

## How to Change the Logo

You can replace AhaSlides logo with your own, or even any other image or GIF you want. It will show at the top-right of each of your slides.

- Navigate to the AhaSlides editor and hover over the logo in the top-right of the presentation canvas.
- Click the logo and select **'Change'**.
- Upload your logo.
- Reset the logo to the original AhaSlides logo by hovering over it and clicking **'Reset'**.

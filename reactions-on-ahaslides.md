# Reactions on AhaSlides

You can let your audience send live reactions during your presentation, so participants can respond in the moment without interrupting the flow.

## How to enable reactions

1. While editing your presentation, go to the **Settings** menu.
2. Click **Other settings**.
3. Choose which reactions your audience can send during the presentation: Like, Love, Haha, Wow, and Sad.

Once enabled, your audience can tap the reactions you selected on their devices, and the reactions appear live during your presentation.

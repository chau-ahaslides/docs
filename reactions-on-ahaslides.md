# Reactions on AhaSlides

You can now let your audience send reactions during your presentation

While editing your presentation, go to the **Settings** menu. In there, you will see **Other settings**.

Choose which reaction you want your audience to send during your presentation (Like, Love, Haha, Wow, Sad)

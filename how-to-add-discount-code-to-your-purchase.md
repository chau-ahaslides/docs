# How to add discount code to your purchase?

**1. Choose Your Plan**
Select the plan you'd like to purchase and proceed to the **Checkout** page.

**2. Add the Promotion Code**
On the Checkout page, locate the **'Add promotion code'** text right under the **'Subtotal'** section. Click on it.

**3. Enter the Code**
Enter your discount code in the field that appears, then click **'Apply'**.

title: How to add discount code to your purchase?
status: Published
---

- **Choose Your Plan**: Select the plan you'd like to purchase and proceed to the **Checkout** page.
- **Add the Promotion Code**: On the Checkout page, locate the **'Add promotion code'** text right under the **'Subtotal'** section. Click on it.
- **Enter the Code**: Enter your discount code in the field that appears, then click **'Apply'**.

## The Log out everywhere function on AhaSlides will log you out from all other devices and browsers.


### Here's how:

- While in your AhaSlides account, go to [**My profile**](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-profile)
- Choose **Log out everywhere else**
- Press **Log out** - This will log you out from all other devices and browsers.

A Monthly plan is an AhaSlides plan that is **auto-renewed every month**.

If you want to use a monthly plan for just one month, you can buy the plan and then **[turn off auto-renewal](https://help.ahaslides.com/portal/en/kb/articles/cancelling-your-ahaslides-subscription)** (cancel plan). To see all available Monthly plans, please check out the **Monthly tab** on the Pricing page: [Pricing & Plans | Flexible & Great Value | AhaSlides](https://ahaslides.com/pricing/).

After purchasing a Monthly plan, you can **turn off auto-renewal before the renewal date** by using this guide: [Cancelling your AhaSlides Subscription](https://help.ahaslides.com/portal/en/kb/articles/cancelling-your-ahaslides-subscription), to avoid being charged for your second month.

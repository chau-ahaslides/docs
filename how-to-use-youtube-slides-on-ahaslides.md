# How to Use YouTube Slides on AhaSlides

## AhaSlides YouTube Slides let you play YouTube videos on both the presenter and audience screen.

### Here's how:

1. Choose the Youtube video that you want to play on AhaSlides
2. Copy that YouTube link
3. Paste the link of the Youtube video you want to insert to **YouTube link**

Remember to check the box **Also show on audience's smartphones** if you want to show the YouTube video to be shown on the audience's smartphones.

*Please note that your audience will need to click the Play button on the video themselves.*

**Pro Tip:** To embed a YouTube Short into AhaSlides, follow these steps:

1. Open the YouTube Shorts video.
2. Click on the URL in the browser's address bar.
3. Replace the word **"shorts"** with **"watch"** in the URL.
4. Press Enter.

*The Shorts link will be converted into a regular YouTube watch link, which you can then embed into AhaSlides.*

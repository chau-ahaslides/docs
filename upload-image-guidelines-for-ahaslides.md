# Upload image guidelines for AhaSlides

For the best image quality on AhaSlides, follow the guidelines below.

## Supported image formats

AhaSlides supports JPEG, PNG, and GIF images. After upload, images are automatically optimised and converted to .webm format to improve performance and playback speed.

## Maximum file size

The maximum upload file size is 15 MB.

## Image dimensions and aspect ratio

Images look best when their aspect ratio closely matches your presentation screen ratio. The standard screen ratios are:

- **16:9**, the most common
- **4:3**

You can use other dimensions, but matching your presentation ratio gives the best visual results.

## Resolution recommendations

For full-screen background images, use 2K resolution (2560 x 1440). This keeps the display sharp while maintaining smooth performance, even on large screens or projectors. The maximum supported resolution after processing is 2560 x 1440 (2K).

Original-quality background rendering is not supported.

## Image compression and quality

All uploaded images are automatically optimised to keep presentations running smoothly. There is no option to disable compression or preserve original quality after upload.

## Presentation rendering

During playback, presentations are rendered locally on the presenter's device rather than streamed from AhaSlides servers. This helps keep performance stable and responsive during live sessions.

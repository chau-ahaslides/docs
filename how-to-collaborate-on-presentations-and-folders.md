Collaborative editing lets you work together on presentations or folders with other AhaSlides users.

- **Collaborating with team members in your team plan** is available on **all plans**.
- **Collaborating with individuals outside your team** is supported on **Pro, Edu Large, and Enterprise plans**.
Please note that **features of the owner's plan/subscription will not be shared through the collaboration feature**.


# Adding your Collaborators


## 1. Add Collaborators to your Presentation

There are two ways to add collaborators to your presentation:

- **In the presentation editor toolbar** - You'll see an icon containing your profile image and a '+' icon
- You can also click on the **Share** menu button and choose **Add Collaborators** tab
Clicking either of these will bring up the invitation menu. From here, you can enter your collaborators' email addresses and choose **'Editor'** or **'Presenter'** role for them.

You can also invite **Editors** using the **Invite editors via link** option in the **Share** menu.


## 2. Add Collaborators to your Folder

- From the My Presentation view, locate and select the folder you wish to share.
- Click on the **3-dot button** positioned to the right of the selected folder and choose '**Add Collaborators**'
- In the provided field, type the email addresses of the collaborators, and select a role for them. After that, click the '**Add**' button to confirm.

# Editing and Viewing

For now, **it is not yet possible for two people to edit a presentation at the same time**. While your collaborators have permission to edit and present the same presentation, there can only be **one person actively editing or presenting the presentation at any given time**.


# Finding Shared Presentations or Folders on the Dashboard

As the **owner**, any presentation or folder that you share with a collaborator will still appear in the 'My Presentations' tab. As an **added collaborator**, any presentations or folders that you've been invited to collaborate on will appear in the 'Shared with Me' tab.

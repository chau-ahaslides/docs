Collaborative editing lets you work together on presentations or folders with other AhaSlides users.

> **Info:** Whether you're collaborating within your team or with people outside it, here's how it works:
>
> - **Collaborating with team members in your team plan** is available on **all plans**, allowing smooth teamwork regardless of your subscription level. More info on team can be found here: [Managing your Team Members](managing-your-team-members.md)
> - **Collaborating with individuals outside your team** is supported on **Pro, Edu Large, and Enterprise plans**. However, for a limited time, **all users** can enjoy external collaboration as part of a special promotional offer.

> **Note:** Please note that **features of the owner's plan/subscription will not be shared through the collaboration feature**.
>
> If you require a plan for multiple users, we recommend checking out our Team Plan: [Purchasing Multiple Licenses for AhaSlides Team Plan Subscription](purchasing-multiple-licenses-for-ahaslides-team-plan-subscription.md)

# Adding your Collaborators

## 1. Add Collaborators to your Presentation

There are two ways to add collaborators to your presentation:

- **In the presentation editor toolbar** - You'll see an icon containing your profile image and a '+' icon

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsne86bf33f1ed70438f5387e36095c933f7fab13f182eac60372e1664b22767bd2aaad35fe6534ed5ec54cae0f8c4474b8?inline=true)

- You can also click on the **Share** menu button and choose **Add Collaborators** tab

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn27a2d68d66a7840cccea56f00ba364dc96cf52b2dcfa33529d719c140b320310bd7e101e5896f5c0a18a67f63d2f0a37?inline=true)

Clicking either of these will bring up the invitation menu. From here, you can enter your collaborators' email addresses and choose **'Editor'** or **'Presenter'** role for them.

> **Note:** You can find out more about the **Presenter** role here: [Multiple Presenters, One Presentation with the Presenter Role](presenter-role.md)

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn5f18abc537f91f2684cb33483ed8d1e96e6b63a7632aa873255dc7961f37a4287f8fa0c2b9b9e3c367c01f58b7017fe9?inline=true)

Once added, your collaborators will be listed in this menu alongside a dropdown box where you can **remove them as a collaborator**.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn3e9081a6115f2c1932b88d3517f3e21552e20d4f4a48ca1abb8ed5f7a424e9f382e24fd94aeb2a12bdf54b59f483954a?inline=true)

Your added collaborators will receive an email informing them that they've been given editing rights in your presentation. You will always remain the owner of the presentation.

You can also invite **Editors** using the **Invite editors via link** option in the **Share** menu (note: this feature **does not support** the **Presenter** role).

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnded9acf142606c175867136342b7b7fb303a639cc50ff18770eded02c1c576572796186d1a754bc86a12228f71cf8baa?inline=true)

To use it:

- Turn on the Invite editors via link **toggle**.
- A shareable link will appear. Click Copy to copy the link.
- Send the link to your collaborators.

When recipients open the link, they will be prompted to sign in (if they are not already). After signing in, they will gain access to your presentation as an **Editor**.

## 2. Add Collaborators to your Folder

a. From the [**My Presentation**](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/presentations) view, locate and select the folder you wish to share.

b. Click on the **3-dot** **button** positioned to the right of the selected folder (or right-click on the folder) and choose '**Add Collaborators**'

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn7b12ff131fb076c0052745251dd2eb33d660a99649b7de35accc9b750bee48d6e9e88809b0e99c6cbf647facb140507f?inline=true)

c. In the provided field, type the email addresses of the collaborators, and select a role for them. After that, click the '**Add**' button to confirm and grant access to them.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn0fc1e329adc9660922813716f8fdc3d77980a3784d07297e0966eabb4d99befd9da9d4d9c307cbdbbf90a14c64912002?inline=true)

The chosen collaborators will then have access to the shared folder.

# Editing and Viewing

For now, **it is not yet possible for two people to edit a presentation at the same time**. While your collaborators have permission to edit and present the same presentation, there can only be **one person actively editing or presenting the presentation at any given time**.

Your collaborators can view the presentation while it is being edited. When your collaborators are viewing the same presentation, their profile picture will show up in the top toolbar beside the 'Invite' button.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsna3d39648971aed93c8a3532e588c8b0622136d0cada30609d618c606d69b9bff0f6f007c13e8b5652d4a97868a75b7bd?inline=true)

When there is one person editing and one person viewing the presentation, the one who is viewing will see a bar appearing at the top of the editor:

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsna3d39648971aed93c8a3532e588c8b06a271dede0d7904457679ac3a450f87ace0354220014439400d4c6761c9978eca?inline=true)

Users seeing this bar will have two options:

1. **Take over -** Pressing 'Take over' will immediately make you the editor of the presentation and will turn whoever is currently the editor into a viewer.

2. **Refresh -** Pressing 'Refresh' will refresh the page so that you can view any new changes made by the editor.

As long as there is no one currently editing or presenting the presentation, any collaborator can press the **Present** button to start presenting that presentation immediately (doing this will take over the presentation).

# Finding Shared Presentations or Folders on the Dashboard

As the **owner**, any presentation or folder that you share with a collaborator will still appear in the 'My Presentations' tab with the rest of the presentations you've created.
As an **added collaborator**, any presentations or folders that you've been invited to collaborate on will appear in the 'Shared with Me' tab.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn88b9ede080c27d8c5000a00c15348d123a6622fa0608f1bcb00286c07e6ee0e0c652b14ba482874c31751c8662ad2836?inline=true)

From here, any added collaborator can **Leave** a presentation/folder that they've been invited to edit by right-clicking on them (or clicking on the 3-dot button on the right side), then choose **'Leave'**

# Moving Presentations across Folders

> **Note:** You can move any presentation to any folder if you have **edit access** to **all of the following**:
>
> - The source folder of the presentation.
> - The presentation.
> - The destination folder (whether it's yours or shared by others).
>
> Therefore, if you would like your collaborators to be able to move your presentations, you would need to:
>
> - Move your presentations into a folder (yours or shared by others)
> - Invite them to collaborate on that folder as "Editor".
>
> **Note: Only presentations that you created** (you are the owner of) **can be moved to** the **root folder** of your accounts' **My Presentation** page.

## To move a presentation

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnc43dfe8b3546f66f9b64f1cd305699a64208733b6ed8e6365458de8a032d704517769b2883026236d18bad3ecc0053ff?inline=true)

1. Click the **check box** next to the thumbnail of the presentation (you can also select multiple presentations), this box will only be available for presentations that you are able to move. Then, click **"Move to"** on the **tool bar**, which appears at the bottom of the page.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn582e46407d8c48527758dc30b8e26dc197dfc8df87681b666fa4a623241715b84528f08c0c11cedc1c0661e856addf36?inline=true)

You can also **right-click** on a presentation, or click the ![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnc43dfe8b3546f66f9b64f1cd305699a6ef045ed90085ba7e0e383d40aa31ec16b2a126959c4a3f8cf170253d37c477db?inline=true) button to show the **option menu**. In there, you can find the **"Move to"** option.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn8c2d08e2bf074f5933bbff693caf97a828d61063cb479ed55505a458ce44e34261dd9ec15e47af9f0d4d3dfc696942a8?inline=true)

2. Choose the folder and click **"Move"**.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsn30181b26512ce29f825741a7f141757176955b498a74c1db943b12cd092258ebb09bfae77c877c461b70a4226d991f24?inline=true)

3. There are 2 cases:

- If you move the presentation from a private folder to another private folder: The presentation will be moved to the destination folder immediately.
- For other cases: a message about changing access rights will pop up. If you would like to proceed, click **"Move"**. The presentation will then be moved to the destination folder.

![](https://help.ahaslides.com/galleryDocuments/edbsnc43dfe8b3546f66f9b64f1cd305699a65cc71f783793aa3ec2b010d69ba91d7da4ea0a837353ce791012b423f8617629?inline=true)

# Billing information and Invoice/Receipt

At AhaSlides, we make it convenient for customers to access and download invoices and receipts directly from our website.

> **Note:** To receive your invoices with the correct information, please **update your billing details before making the payment.**
> Changes you make will only show up on new invoices. Old invoices will still retain the information when they were created.

> **Note:** Our company is a **non-GST registered** company - we don't collect any tax from our customers. Therefore, **we don't have tax invoices**.

## Change Billing information

1. Log in to your AhaSlides account.
2. Click on your profile image and select '**Billing**'

Or go to the [My Plan](https://presenter.ahaslides.com/apps/user-plan) page, then click the '**Manage**' button

3. On the billing page, update your information:
- Scroll down to the **'Billing information'** section, then click on '**Update Information**'

- Fill in the required billing details, then scroll down and click '**Save**' to confirm the changes.

## Download Invoice or Receipt with Billing information

Once you've updated your billing information, you can effortlessly download your invoices or receipts:

1. Navigate to your '**Invoice History**' and click on the specific invoice you want to download.

2. You'll be redirected to a page where you can choose to download either your invoice or receipt.

We hope this guide simplifies your billing management on AhaSlides. If you encounter any challenges or have further questions, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.

At AhaSlides, we make it convenient for customers to access and download invoices and receipts directly from our website.

To receive your invoices with the correct information, please **update your billing details before making the payment.** Changes you make will only show up on new invoices.

Our company is a **non-GST registered** company - we don't collect any tax from our customers. Therefore, **we don't have tax invoices**.


## Change Billing information

- Log in to your AhaSlides account.
- Click on your profile image and select '**Billing**'
- On the billing page, update your information: Scroll down to the **'Billing information'** section, then click on '**Update Information**'
- Fill in the required billing details, then scroll down and click '**Save**' to confirm the changes.

## Download Invoice or Receipt with Billing information

- Navigate to your '**Invoice History**' and click on the specific invoice you want to download.
- You'll be redirected to a page where you can choose to download either your invoice or receipt.
